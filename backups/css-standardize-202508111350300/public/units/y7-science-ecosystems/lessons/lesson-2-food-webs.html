Lesson 2: Food Chains & Food Webs

Modelling Energy Flow

  1. Introduce producers, consumers, decomposers; link to local species (e.g., harakeke, tuna/eel, manu).
  2. Students build 3 food chains using picture cards; combine into a class food web.
  3. Discuss stability and what happens if one taonga species declines.

Mātauranga Connection

Consider seasonal knowledge (maramataka) and traditional harvesting practices that maintain balance.

Assessment Task (Formative)

  • Students annotate their food web diagram and write a short explanation of one interdependence pathway.
  • Use rubric criteria: scientific accuracy, clarity, cultural connection.
