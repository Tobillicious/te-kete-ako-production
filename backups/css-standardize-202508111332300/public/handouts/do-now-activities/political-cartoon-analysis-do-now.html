    What is a Political Cartoon?

    A political cartoon is a drawing that makes a point about a political issue or event. Cartoonists use symbols, exaggeration, and humor to get their message across quickly and powerfully. Your task is to act like a detective, uncovering the layers of meaning in the cartoon below.

    Analysis Questions

    1. Observe: What are the key objects, people, or symbols you see in the cartoon? List at least three.

    2. Interpret: What is the main issue being discussed in the cartoon? What do you think the cartoonist's opinion is on this issue?

    3. Analyze: What techniques does the cartoonist use to make their point? (e.g., exaggeration, irony, symbolism). Give one example.
