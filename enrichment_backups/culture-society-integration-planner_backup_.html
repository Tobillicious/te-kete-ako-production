üåø Cultural Safety Reminder

As you design your society's culture:

  • Draw inspiration from cultural learning, but create something new - don't copy or appropriate
  • Be respectful when referencing Indigenous governance principles you've learned about
  • Include diverse cultural perspectives from your group members' backgrounds
  • Focus on values and principles rather than surface-level cultural symbols
  • Consider how your cultural design impacts all members of your society
