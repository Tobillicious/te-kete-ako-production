📚 Complete Unit Plans

Strategic educational units containing curriculum alignment, cultural context, and pedagogical justification. Each unit houses multiple 75-minute lesson plans.

🌟 Unit 1: Te Ao Māori - Cultural Identity & Knowledge Systems

NEW! 6-8 weeks exploring Māori worldviews as foundation for all learning. Years 7-10. Full cultural authenticity with cross-curricular connections.

🔥 Unit 2: Decolonized Aotearoa History

NEW! 8-10 weeks of counter-narratives centering Māori agency from pre-colonial excellence to contemporary sovereignty. Years 8-12.

🌊 Unit 3: STEM + Mātauranga Māori

NEW! 8-10 weeks integrating traditional and modern science knowledge. Dual knowledge systems for environmental action and innovation.

💰 Unit 4: Economic Justice

NEW! 6-8 weeks exploring economic systems, equity, and justice from a Te Ao Māori perspective. Years 9-11.

🌏 Unit 5: Global Connections & Critical Media

NEW! 6-8 weeks analyzing media, globalization, and their impacts on culture and identity. Years 9-12.

🚀 Unit 6: Future Rangatiratanga & Action

NEW! 4-6 week project-based unit where students lead change in their communities. Years 10-13.

🤖 Unit 7: Digital Technologies & AI Ethics

NEW! 4-6 week senior unit on the principles and ethics of AI, including prompt engineering and Māori data sovereignty.

Year 8: How Systems Shape Our Lives

Complete 5-week unit exploring government systems, rights, and civic participation. (Legacy - being enhanced)

Writer's Toolkit Unit

Comprehensive writing skills unit covering techniques from show-don't-tell to rhetorical devices. (Being culturally enhanced)

Unit 4: Contemporary Issues & Critical Literacy (Planned)

6-8 weeks analyzing current issues through cultural and postcolonial lens. Years 9-13.
