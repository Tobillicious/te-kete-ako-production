Unit Overview

This unit empowers students to imagine and mathematically model alternative futures rooted in justice, equity, and Indigenous values. Students use mathematical tools to design and analyze pathways toward decolonized systems in education, governance, and community wellbeing.

Learning Objectives

Mathematics (NZ Curriculum Level 5-6)

  • Modeling: Create mathematical models of alternative social systems
  • Optimization: Use math to find optimal pathways to goals
  • Prediction: Forecast outcomes of different policy choices
  • Systems Thinking: Understand interconnections in complex systems

Decolonization & Futures

  • Imagine alternatives to colonial systems
  • Use Indigenous values as design principles
  • Create actionable pathways to equity and justice
  • Develop agency and leadership for systemic change
