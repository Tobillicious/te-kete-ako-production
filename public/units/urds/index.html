📋 Unit Requirements Documents

URDs are detailed planning specifications that outline the vision, objectives, resources, and implementation strategies for complete curriculum units. They serve as blueprints for developing comprehensive, culturally-responsive learning experiences.

Each URD centers Māori perspectives and ensures authentic integration of mātauranga Māori throughout the learning journey.
