📋 Unit Requirements Documents

Comprehensive planning documents for developing culturally-responsive, decolonized curriculum units

📖 What are Unit Requirements Documents?

URDs are detailed planning specifications that outline the vision, objectives, resources, and implementation strategies for complete curriculum units. They serve as blueprints for developing comprehensive, culturally-responsive learning experiences.

Each URD centers Māori perspectives and ensures authentic integration of mātauranga Māori throughout the learning journey.

📚 Available Planning Documents

Unit 2: Decolonized Aotearoa History

A comprehensive, evidence-based learning experience that centers Māori perspectives, agency, and resistance in the history of Aotearoa New Zealand. Challenges colonial narratives and equips students to critically analyze historical roots of contemporary society.

Key Topics:

  • Pre-colonial Māori innovation and technology
  • New Zealand Wars from Māori resistance perspective
  • Long-term impacts of colonization
  • 20th-century Māori activism
  • Contemporary Tino Rangatiratanga movements
