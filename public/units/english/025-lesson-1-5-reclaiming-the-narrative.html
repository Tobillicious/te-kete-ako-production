    Lesson 1.5: Reclaiming the Narrative - Ka Whawhai Tonu Mātou

    Unit: Walker - The Challenge to the Narrative
    Year Level: Year 10
    Subject: Social Studies, History, English
    Duration: 75 minutes

    📖 Overview

    This culminating lesson explores Dr. Ranginui Walker's landmark book Ka Whawhai Tonu Mātou: Struggle Without End (1990), which challenged the dominant Pākehā narrative of New Zealand history. Students will reflect on why telling our own stories matters and how historical narratives shape identity and justice.

    🎯 Learning Intention

    We are learning to understand how Dr. Ranginui Walker reclaimed Māori history through Ka Whawhai Tonu Mātou.
