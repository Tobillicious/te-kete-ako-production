    Te Ao Māori Unit

    Explore mātauranga Māori across the curriculum - integrating traditional knowledge with contemporary learning

    Unit Overview

    This unit explores Te Ao Māori - the Māori worldview - integrated across diverse curriculum areas. Students engage with contemporary topics through the lens of mātauranga Māori, connecting traditional knowledge systems with modern challenges and opportunities.
