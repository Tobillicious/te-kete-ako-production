Year 9 Science: Aotearoa's Living Landscapes

Unit Duration: 6 Weeks | Level: NZC Level 5 (Science: Living World)

Ngā Taonga o Te Taiao - The Treasures of Our Environment

Lesson 1: NZ Ecosystems & Whakapapa

Focus: Understanding ecosystem components and native species interactions through Māori perspectives.

  • 🎥 Watch "Ngā Taonga o Te Taiao" (DOC biodiversity video)
  • 🕸️ Hands-on Lab: "Who Eats Whom?" - NZ food web construction
  • 💻 Digital Game: EcoChains NZ Edition (trophic cascades)
  • 🌿 Cultural Connection: Whakapapa (interconnectedness) discussion
Assessment: Food web diagram with ecosystem explanations

Lesson 2: Biodiversity & Endemism

Focus: Why Aotearoa has unique species and what threatens them.

  • 🦎 Case Study: Tuatara as living fossil investigation
  • 🎮 Digital Activity: NZ Biodiversity Tagger game
  • 📋 Field Study Planning: Prepare biodiversity survey
  • 🏞️ Cultural Learning: Ngāhere (forest) ecosystems
Assessment: Report on endemic NZ species adaptations

Lesson 3: Field Study - Rangahau Taiao

Focus: Conducting real biodiversity surveys and analyzing ecosystem health.

  • 🔬 Field Trip: Quadrat surveys of plant/invertebrate species
  • 📱 Citizen Science: Upload findings to iNaturalist NZ
  • 🌡️ Abiotic Measurements: Temperature, soil pH testing
  • 🚫 Cultural Protocol: Tapu (sacredness) of species
Assessment: Field report comparing two microhabitats

Lesson 4: Human Impact & Conservation

Focus: Analyzing threats to NZ ecosystems and conservation strategies.

  • 🐨 Case Study: Possum impact on native forests
  • 💻 Interactive Lab: "Predator Free NZ" simulation
  • 🎙️ Guest Speaker: DOC ranger or iwi conservationist
  • ⚖️ Debate: "Should 1080 be used for pest control?"
Assessment: Persuasive letter to DOC on conservation issue

Lesson 5: Restoration & Kaitiakitanga

Focus: Ecological restoration through Māori guardianship principles.

  • 🌱 Hands-on Project: Plant native seedlings (harakeke, pōhutukawa)
  • 🎮 Digital Activity: EcoRestore NZ virtual game
  • ⛔ Cultural Learning: Rāhui (temporary bans) for recovery
  • 👥 Group Planning: School ecosystem restoration proposal
Assessment: Local area restoration proposal with budget

Lesson 6: Guardians of the Future

Focus: Synthesizing learning into conservation action campaigns.

  • 🎨 Project Creation: Conservation campaign (poster/video/podcast)
  • 🗣️ Science Wānanga: Present projects to peers
  • 💭 Reflection: Personal environmental action commitments
  • 🙏 Closing: Karakia for the taiao
Assessment: Campaign project + self-reflection journal
