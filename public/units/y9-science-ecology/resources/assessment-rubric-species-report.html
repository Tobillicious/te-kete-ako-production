Native Species Research Report Rubric

For in-depth investigation of one NZ native species (plant, bird, insect, reptile, or fish).

CriteriaExcellent (7-8)Proficient (5-6)Developing (3-4)Emerging (1-2)
Species Information Comprehensive biological data: taxonomy, lifecycle, diet, habitat needs, adaptations; scientifically accurate. Key facts covered; mostly accurate; some detail. Basic facts; some inaccuracies or gaps. Minimal or incorrect information.
Ecological Role Explains species' role in ecosystem; identifies relationships (predator/prey, pollinator, seed disperser); discusses consequences if species declines. Describes role; identifies 2-3 relationships; shows ecosystem awareness. Vague role description; limited relationship analysis. Doesn't explain ecological importance.
Conservation Status & Threats Accurate status (DOC classification); identifies specific threats with evidence; discusses conservation efforts underway. Correct status; names main threats; mentions some conservation work. General threats; limited detail on conservation. Unclear status; vague threats.

Sources & Referencing 5+ credible sources (DOC, iwi websites, scientific journals); correctly cited; diverse perspectives. 3-4 sources; mostly credible; basic citation. 1-2 sources; weak citation. No sources or unreliable (Wikipedia only).
Presentation Professional layout; compelling images with captions; error-free; engaging to read. Clear organisation; good images; minor errors. Basic layout; few images; several errors. Poor organisation; inadequate visuals.

Scoring: 48 points total

42-48 = A | 30-41 = B | 18-29 = C | 6-17 = D/E (revise)
