Persuasive Letter: Conservation Advocacy

Write a formal letter to decision-makers advocating for ecosystem protection. Use evidence + kaitiakitanga reasoning.

Letter Structure Guide

  1. Salutation: Address the recipient formally (Hon. Minister, Mayor, Councillor)
  2. Introduction: State your purpose clearly
  3. Body (3 paragraphs): Evidence, kaitiakitanga perspective, call to action
  4. Conclusion: Summarize and request specific outcome
  5. Closing: Yours sincerely, [name]
