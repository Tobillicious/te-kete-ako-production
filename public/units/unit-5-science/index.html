Unit 5: Climate Justice & Leadership

Climate Science & Indigenous Solutions

"Toitū te marae a Tāne, toitū te marae a Tangaroa, toitū te tangata"

If the land is well and the sea is well, the people will thrive

Unit Overview

This unit uses climate science to understand global environmental change and its disproportionate impacts on Indigenous peoples. Students explore Indigenous-led climate solutions and develop leadership skills for environmental stewardship rooted in kaitiakitanga.

3Lessons
9-12Hours
4Resources
1Assessment

Learning Objectives

Science (NZ Curriculum Level 5-6)

  • Climate Science: Understand greenhouse effect and climate systems
  • Ecology: Explore ecosystem responses to climate change
  • Data Analysis: Interpret climate data and trends
  • Solutions: Evaluate climate mitigation strategies

Climate Justice & Leadership

  • Understand disproportionate climate impacts
  • Explore Indigenous climate solutions
  • Apply kaitiakitanga to climate action
  • Develop environmental leadership skills
