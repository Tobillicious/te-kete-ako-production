Waka Physics Basics

Examine the physics of waka (traditional canoes) and ocean navigation. Explore buoyancy, hydrodynamics, wave motion, and force balance that enabled Polynesian voyagers to cross vast oceans. Calculate displacement, analyze hull design for stability, and understand how traditional builders applied sophisticated physics without formal scientific frameworks.
