    Physics Of Traditional Games

    Discover physics principles through traditional Māori games like Mū Tōrere, Poi Rakau, and Tī Rākau (stick games). Analyze motion, force, energy transfer, and projectile dynamics embedded in games passed down through generations. Understand how play and learning were connected through physical science—making abstract physics concepts tangible and fun.
