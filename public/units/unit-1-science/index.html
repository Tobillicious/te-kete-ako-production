Unit 1: Ko Wai Au?

Identity & Cultural Expression Through Science

"Ko au te awa, ko te awa ko au"

I am the river, and the river is me

Unit Overview

This unit explores personal and cultural identity through scientific investigation, examining the biology of voice, physics of cultural expression (like haka), and the science of connection. Students investigate how identity is expressed through physical, cultural, and social means.

3Lessons
9-12Hours
4Resources
1Assessment

Learning Objectives

Science (NZ Curriculum Level 5-6)

  • Physics: Understand sound waves, resonance, and energy transfer
  • Biology: Explore human physiology of voice and expression
  • Scientific Inquiry: Design and conduct investigations
  • Systems: Recognize interconnections in identity and expression

Cultural Integration

  • Understand haka as scientific and cultural expression
  • Explore whakapapa as biological and cultural concept
  • Value cultural practices through scientific lens
  • Develop identity as scientist and cultural being
