Unit Overview

This unit uses mathematics to understand climate change impacts, environmental degradation, and the effectiveness of Indigenous climate solutions. Students analyze climate data, model environmental change, and explore how kaitiakitanga (guardianship) principles offer pathways to climate justice.

Learning Objectives

Mathematics (NZ Curriculum Level 5-6)

  • Data Analysis: Interpret climate data and environmental trends
  • Modeling: Create mathematical models of climate change
  • Rates of Change: Analyze environmental degradation rates
  • Prediction: Use mathematical tools for environmental forecasting

Climate Justice & Leadership

  • Understand disproportionate climate impacts on Indigenous peoples
  • Explore Indigenous climate solutions through mathematical frameworks
  • Apply kaitiakitanga principles to environmental mathematics
  • Develop leadership skills for climate action
