    He Whakamōhiotanga | Introduction

    This resource explores how calculus can be applied to model and understand environmental systems, with a focus on integrating mātauranga Māori (Māori knowledge) and traditional ecological practices. By combining mathematical modeling with indigenous wisdom, we can develop more holistic approaches to environmental management.

    Cultural Context

    Traditional Māori environmental practices were based on deep observation and understanding of natural systems over generations. Concepts like kaitiakitanga (guardianship) and whakapapa (genealogical connections) reflect sophisticated ecological knowledge that can be enhanced through mathematical modeling.

    Ngā Whāinga Ako | Learning Objectives

    Horopaki Ahurea | Cultural Context

    Traditional Māori resource management was guided by principles that ensured sustainability:

    • Rāhui: Temporary restrictions on resource use to allow regeneration
    • Manaaki whenua: Caring for the land as a living entity
    • Whakapapa: Understanding interconnected relationships in ecosystems
    • Kaitiakitanga

    Cultural Safety Considerations

    When integrating mātauranga Māori:

    • Respect that some knowledge may be tapu (sacred/restricted)
    • Acknowledge the source of traditional knowledge where appropriate
    • Recognize that mathematical models complement rather than replace traditional knowledge
    • Ensure Māori perspectives are represented accurately and respectfully
    • Consult with local iwi (tribes) when applying these concepts to specific locations

    Ngā Whakamahinga | Applications

    📈 Rāhui Timing Optimization

    Use calculus to determine optimal timing for implementing rāhui (temporary bans) on harvesting resources.

    Mathematical Focus: Optimization using derivatives

    Cultural Connection: Traditional practice of seasonal restrictions

    🌊 Water Flow in Traditional Systems

    Model water flow through traditional kōhanga (nursery) systems using integration.

    Mathematical Focus: Definite integrals and accumulation

    Cultural Connection: Traditional aquaculture practices

    🌿 Plant Regrowth After Harvest

    Analyze plant regrowth patterns using differential equations.

    Mathematical Focus: Differential equations and exponential growth

    Cultural Connection: Sustainable harvesting of rongoā (medicinal plants)

    🐟 Fish Population Dynamics

    Apply logistic growth models to traditional fishing practices.

    Mathematical Focus: Logistic differential equations

    Cultural Connection: Kaitiakitanga of marine resources
