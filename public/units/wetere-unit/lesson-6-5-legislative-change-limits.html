Legislative Change: What Laws Can and Cannot Do

Unit: Wētere - The Minister and the Mandate | Lesson 6.5

Year 10 | Social Studies, Civics | 75 minutes

🎯 WALT: Critically evaluate the power and limitations of legislative change for achieving justice

✅ What Laws CAN Do

  • Provide legal protections (e.g., te reo official status)
  • Return tangible assets (land, money, fisheries)
  • Create institutions (e.g., Māori Language Commission)
  • Official government apologies
  • Establish processes for ongoing justice

❌ What Laws CANNOT Do

  • Bring back ancestors who died from land loss consequences
  • Undo cultural knowledge already lost
  • Eliminate all racism and prejudice
  • Fully restore what was taken (settlements are partial)
  • Change hearts and minds automatically
