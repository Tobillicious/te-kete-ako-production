📖 Unit Overview

This award-winning unit integrates kaitiakitanga (guardianship), manaakitanga (care), and whanaungatanga (relationships) with digital literacy, online safety, and ethical technology use.

🎯 Learning Outcomes

  • Understand digital citizenship and kaitiakitanga
  • Apply Te Whare Tapa Whā to digital wellbeing
  • Recognize online risks and protective strategies
  • Practice upstander behavior and digital manaakitanga
  • Understand data sovereignty and privacy rights
  • Create digital content with cultural integrity

🌟 Unit Structure

📘 Phase 1: Digital Wellbeing (Lessons 1-6)

📗 Phase 2: Digital Safety (Lessons 7-15)

📙 Phase 3: Creation & Showcase (Lessons 16-18)
