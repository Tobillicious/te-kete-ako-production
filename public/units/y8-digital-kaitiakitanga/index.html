    Year 8 Digital Kaitiakitanga

    Digital Guardianship - Becoming responsible stewards of our digital world

    Unit Overview

    This unit explores digital kaitiakitanga (guardianship) - the responsibility to protect and care for digital spaces, information, and communities. Students learn to navigate the digital world with integrity, applying mātauranga Māori values of kaitiakitanga, manaakitanga, and whanaungatanga to their online lives.

    Key Learning Areas

    • Digital citizenship and online responsibility
    • Privacy, security, and data protection
    • Cultural identity in digital spaces
    • Ethical technology use and creation
    • Digital wellbeing and balance
