Y9 English: Progressive Writing Chain

Master writing through a culturally-integrated 5-lesson sequence—from research skills to persuasive argumentation, all grounded in mātauranga Māori frameworks.

5-Lesson Sequence Quality 92-94 Cultural Integration 90%+

🎯 Learning Journey | Te Huarahi Ako

Progressive Skill Development: Research → Creative Writing → Narrative Structures → Poetry Analysis → Argumentative Writing

🌿 Cultural Framework

Each lesson integrates:

  • Mātauranga Māori: Traditional knowledge systems
  • Whakataukī: Proverb-inspired wisdom
  • Pūrākau: Story structures and oral traditions
  • Te Reo Māori: Language integration throughout
