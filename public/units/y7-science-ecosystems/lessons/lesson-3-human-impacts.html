Lesson 3: Human Impacts & Kaitiaki Action

Inquiry Focus

Investigate a local issue (e.g., water quality, invasive species, habitat loss). Gather evidence from field notes and community voices.

Data to Decision

  1. Summarise evidence (tables/graphs, photos, observations).
  2. Identify likely causes and affected species (use food web from L2).
  3. Draft an action plan guided by kaitiakitanga (who, what, when, tikanga).

Action Proposal (Summative)

  • One-page proposal + short presentation to class/wh훮nau.
  • Criteria: scientific validity, feasibility, cultural integrity, positive impact.
