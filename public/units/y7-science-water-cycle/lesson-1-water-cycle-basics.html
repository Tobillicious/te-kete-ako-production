The 4 Stages:

  1. Evaporation: Sun heats water ‚Üí turns to vapor (gas)
  2. Condensation: Vapor cools ‚Üí forms clouds (tiny water droplets)
  3. Precipitation: Clouds release water (rain, snow, hail)
  4. Collection: Water gathers in oceans, rivers, lakes ‚Üí cycle repeats!
Activity: Draw and label complete water cycle diagram. Include: sun (energy source), ocean, clouds, rain, rivers, arrows showing movement.
