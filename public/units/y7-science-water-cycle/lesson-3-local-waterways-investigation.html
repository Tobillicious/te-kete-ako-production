Lesson 3: Local Waterways Investigation

🔬 Investigation Guide

Option A: Field Study (if safe access available)

  1. Observe water clarity, flow speed, odor
  2. Test pH, temperature (if equipment available)
  3. Identify plants/animals present
  4. Note pollution sources (visible rubbish, pipes, etc.)
  5. Take photos, measurements, notes

Option B: Research Project

  1. Name & history of local waterway
  2. Which iwi are kaitiaki?
  3. Water quality reports (council website)
  4. Threats to waterway health
  5. Current conservation efforts
Deliverable: Poster or presentation (5 min) showing findings. Include iwi connection, water health status, and one recommendation for improvement.
