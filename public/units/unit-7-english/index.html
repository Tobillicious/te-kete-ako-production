Unit Overview

This culminating unit brings together all writing craft skills from Units 1-6. Students revise and polish their work, creating a comprehensive portfolio that demonstrates mastery of narrative, argumentative, analytical, and creative writing while showcasing their development as writers and critical thinkers.

Learning Objectives

Writing Craft (NZ Curriculum Level 5-6)

  • Revision: Apply systematic revision strategies to polish writing
  • Reflection: Analyze growth as a writer across multiple genres
  • Integration: Synthesize multiple writing skills in one piece
  • Portfolio Development: Curate and present best work professionally

Synthesis & Impact

  • Demonstrate mastery across writing modes
  • Integrate cultural knowledge with writing craft
  • Create meaningful writing for authentic audiences
  • Develop identity as confident, skilled writer

Culminating Assessment

Comprehensive Writing Portfolio

Students demonstrate mastery through a curated portfolio that includes:

  • Personal narrative (Unit 1 - hooks, show don't tell)
  • Argumentative essay (Unit 2 - PEEL, rhetorical devices)
  • Poetry analysis (Unit 3 - diction, tone, dual frameworks)
  • Media critique (Unit 4 - informative writing, critical analysis)
  • Persuasive piece (Unit 5 - suspense, fluency)
  • Vision statement (Unit 6 - powerful conclusions)
  • Reflection essay analyzing growth as writer

All pieces revised and polished to demonstrate mastery.
