Unit Overview

This culminating unit brings together all mathematical concepts, cultural knowledge, and critical thinking skills from Units 1-6. Students design and execute a comprehensive mathematics project that addresses a real-world issue in their community, demonstrating mastery while creating actual impact.

Learning Objectives

Mathematics (NZ Curriculum Level 5-6)

  • Integration: Apply multiple mathematical concepts in one project
  • Inquiry: Design and conduct mathematical investigations
  • Communication: Present mathematical findings professionally
  • Application: Use mathematics to solve real community problems

Synthesis & Impact

  • Integrate learning from all previous units
  • Create meaningful community impact
  • Demonstrate cultural competency and mathematical mastery
  • Develop agency as mathematical thinkers and change-makers
