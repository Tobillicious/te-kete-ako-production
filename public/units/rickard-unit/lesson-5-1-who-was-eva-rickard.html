🎯 WALT: Understand Eva Rickard's journey from farmer's wife to national land rights leader

👩‍🌾 From Ordinary to Extraordinary

Tuaiwa (Eva) Rickard (1925-1997) - Tainui Awhiro iwi, Raglan

  • Farmer, mother, grandmother - "ordinary" life until 1970s
  • Watched her people's land stolen in 1941 "temporarily" for WWII airfield
  • After war: Government gave land to Raglan Golf Club instead of returning it
  • Rickard decided: Enough. Led 507-day occupation to reclaim Te Kōpua
  • Arrested multiple times, never gave up - land finally returned 1995

💬 Her Famous Quote

"Ka whawhai tonu mātou, ake ake ake!"

We will fight on forever and ever
