Unit Overview

This unit uses science to examine the environmental impacts of different economic systems, exploring resource extraction, pollution, and waste. Students investigate who bears the environmental costs of economic activities and explore sustainable alternatives rooted in kaitiakitanga.

Learning Objectives

Science (NZ Curriculum Level 5-6)

  • Environmental Science: Understand resource extraction impacts
  • Chemistry: Explore pollution and waste
  • Ecology: Investigate ecosystem impacts of economic activity
  • Data Analysis: Analyze environmental justice data
