üîÑ The PPDAC Cycle

PPDAC is the 5-stage cycle statisticians use to investigate questions:

P

PROBLEM - Define your question

What do you want to find out? Be specific! "Do students sleep enough?" ‚Üí "What percentage of Y9 students get 8+ hours of sleep on school nights?"

P

PLAN - Design your investigation

How will you get data? Survey? Experiment? Existing data? Who will you ask? How many people? What questions?

D

DATA - Collect your information

Gather your data systematically. Keep it organized. Record accurately. Check for errors.

A

ANALYSIS - Make sense of the data

Create graphs, calculate averages, look for patterns. What does the data show? Any surprises?

C

CONCLUSION - Answer your question

What did you find out? Does it answer your original question? What recommendations can you make? What further questions arise?
