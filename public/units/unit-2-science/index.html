Unit 2: Decolonized Narratives

Traditional Scientific Knowledge & Indigenous Innovation

"Ehara taku toa i te toa takitahi, engari he toa takitini"

My strength is not mine alone, but that of the collective

Unit Overview

This unit explores sophisticated scientific knowledge in traditional Indigenous practices, challenging colonial narratives about "primitive" peoples. Students investigate traditional navigation, rongoa Māori (traditional medicine), and sustainable resource management through scientific inquiry.

5Lessons
15-20Hours
6Resources
2Assessments

Learning Objectives

Science (NZ Curriculum Level 5-6)

  • Scientific Method: Apply traditional and Western scientific inquiry
  • Astronomy: Understand traditional celestial navigation
  • Biology: Explore rongoa Māori and plant science
  • Ecology: Investigate sustainable resource management
