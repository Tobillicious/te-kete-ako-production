🏙️ Urban Māori Today (2025)

  • 85% of Māori now live in urban areas
  • Thriving urban marae and community centers
  • Kapa haka groups, Kōhanga Reo in cities
  • Urban Māori authorities providing health, education, employment services
  • Cultural identity maintained through urban adaptations
