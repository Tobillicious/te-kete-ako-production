    Nzhistory Investigation Toolkit

    Develop historical research skills using NZ History resources and methodologies. Learn to analyze primary sources, evaluate evidence, construct historical arguments, and recognize different perspectives on past events. Practice inquiry methods that center Māori voices and challenge dominant narratives—becoming critical historians who understand that history is interpretation, not just facts.
