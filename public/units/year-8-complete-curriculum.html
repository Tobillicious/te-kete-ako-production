⚙️ Y8 Systems: Understanding Society (63 lessons)

Comprehensive social studies unit exploring governance, democracy, Te Tiriti, and civic participation.

Unit 1: Society & Systems

Introduction to systems thinking, democracy vs dictatorship, NZ government structure, local governance, and civic participation.

6 core lessons

Unit 2: Te Tiriti & Colonisation

The Treaty of Waitangi as a constitutional system, colonisation impacts, and co-governance models.

Critical NZ context

Unit 3: Power & Governance

Traditional Māori governance, colonial impacts, justice systems, and tikanga in modern law.

Cultural integration

Unit 4: Economics & Sustainability

Economic systems, sustainable development, and environmental justice.

Future-focused

Unit 5: Guided Inquiry Project

Student-led investigation into systems in their community with presentation and reflection.

Authentic assessment
