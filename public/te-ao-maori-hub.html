💡 How to Use These Resources

✓ Seek Cultural Guidance

Consult with kaitiaki, kaumātua, or cultural advisors when teaching sensitive content

✓ Provide Context

Ensure students understand the cultural significance and proper protocols

✓ Integrate Authentically

Avoid tokenistic inclusion - make cultural connections genuine and meaningful

✓ Encourage Student Voice

Create space for Māori students to share their own knowledge and perspectives
