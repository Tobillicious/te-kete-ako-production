    Teacher Implementation Notes

    Timing: Best used in the first 2-3 weeks of Term 1. Allow 3-4 lessons to complete core activities.

    Differentiation:

    • For students needing more support: Pair with buddy, focus on checklist completion
    • For extension: Have students create digital versions of resources to share

    Cultural Considerations:

    • Invite MÄori student leaders to share their transition experiences
    • Connect activities to local iwi knowledge where possible
    • Validate different cultural perspectives on education

    Assessment: Use checklist completion as formative assessment. Pepeha or Survival Guide can be summative tasks.
