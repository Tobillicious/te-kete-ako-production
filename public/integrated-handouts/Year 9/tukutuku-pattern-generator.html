    Poutama (Stairway to Heaven)

    Represents the ascent of TƒÅwhaki to the heavens and the pursuit of knowledge. Mathematically demonstrates geometric progression and stepwise growth.

    • Stepwise growth pattern

    Roimata Toroa (Tears of the Albatross)

    Mathematical Properties:
    • Alternating patterns
    • Reflective symmetry
    • Binary sequences

    Patiki (Flounder)

    Represents abundance and provision. Mathematically demonstrates diamond shapes and rotational symmetry.

    Mathematical Properties:
    • Diamond geometry
    • Rotational symmetry
    • Tessellation patterns

    • Create original patterns
