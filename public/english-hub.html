Pūrākau

Māori storytelling traditions that:

  • Carry cultural values & knowledge
  • Connect past, present, future
  • Use metaphor & symbolism
  • Embed whakapapa & tikanga
  • Serve multiple purposes (teach, entertain, preserve)
