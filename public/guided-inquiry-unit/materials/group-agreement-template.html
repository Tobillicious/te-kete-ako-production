🎯 Creating Our Group Agreement

A group agreement helps ensure everyone feels safe, respected, and able to contribute their best work. This document becomes your team's charter - a living agreement you can return to when challenges arise.

Agreement Building Process:

  1. Each member shares their hopes and concerns for group work
  2. Discuss and agree on specific expectations
  3. Create clear processes for communication and decision-making
  4. Plan how to handle conflicts respectfully
  5. All members sign to show commitment
