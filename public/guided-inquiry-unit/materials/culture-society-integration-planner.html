    📋 Instructions

    Use this planner to thoughtfully integrate cultural values and practices into your society design. Culture isn't just decoration - it shapes how your society makes decisions, resolves conflicts, and builds community.

    Work as a group to make decisions that everyone can support and explain.

    Step 1: Core Cultural Values

    What are the 2-3 most important values that will guide your society? (Think about what you learned from Indigenous governance systems and your own cultural backgrounds)

    Step 2: Values → Governance Connection

    How will your core values influence the way your society makes decisions?

    What decision-making processes will reflect these values? (e.g., consensus, council of elders, community consultation, etc.)

    How will your society handle disagreements or conflicts in a way that honors your values?

    Step 3: Cultural Practices & Traditions

    What cultural practices, ceremonies, or traditions will be important in your society?

    How will these practices strengthen community connections and reinforce your values?

    How will your society pass on cultural knowledge to future generations?

    🌿 Cultural Protocols Planning

    What protocols or guidelines will ensure respectful behavior in your society?

    How will visitors or new members learn about your cultural expectations?

    What role will cultural leaders or knowledge keepers play in your governance?

    🤝 Cultural Diversity & Inclusion

    How will your society honor and include people from different cultural backgrounds?

    What systems will you have to prevent cultural discrimination or bias?

    How will different cultural perspectives be included in decision-making?

    Step 4: Culture + Economics + Rights Integration

    How do your cultural values influence your economic system? (What kind of economy reflects your values?)

    What rights and responsibilities in your society are connected to cultural values?

    How will cultural considerations influence laws and rules in your society?

    🎯 Group Reflection & Next Steps

    What aspects of your cultural integration plan are you most excited about?

    What challenges might you face in implementing these cultural elements?

    How has this planning process changed or strengthened your society design?

    What do you still need to work out before your presentation?

    🌿 Cultural Safety Reminder

    As you design your society's culture:

    • Draw inspiration from cultural learning, but create something new - don't copy or appropriate
    • Be respectful when referencing Indigenous governance principles you've learned about
    • Include diverse cultural perspectives from your group members' backgrounds
    • Focus on values and principles rather than surface-level cultural symbols
    • Consider how your cultural design impacts all members of your society
