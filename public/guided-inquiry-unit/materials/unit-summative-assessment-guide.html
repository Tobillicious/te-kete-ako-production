Grading Scale

  • 85-100 points: Excellence (A) - Exceptional work exceeding expectations
  • 70-84 points: Proficient (B) - Strong work meeting all criteria
  • 50-69 points: Developing (C) - Meets some criteria, room for growth
  • Below 50: Emerging (D/E) - Does not meet criteria; conference with teacher
