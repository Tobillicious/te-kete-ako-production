    🌿 Cultural Safety Reminder

    Important: Indigenous knowledge systems are living traditions that belong to specific communities. We learn about them to:

    • Understand different ways of organizing society
    • Appreciate the wisdom in diverse approaches to governance
    • Consider how these insights might inform our own society design
    • Develop respect for Indigenous ways of knowing

    We do not claim to practice or represent these traditions ourselves.
