## Cultural Context & Safety NotesThis resource has been developed with careful consideration of:1. **Te Tiriti o Waitangi Principles**: Partnership, participation, and protection are embedded throughout2. **Māori Perspectives**: Incorporates te reo Māori, whakataukī (proverbs), and concepts like whanaungatanga3. **Digital Equity Focus**: Acknowledges and addresses the digital divide affecting Māori, Pasifika, and rural communities4. **Cultural Safety**: Provides guidance on respectful use of Māori concepts in digital spaces## NZ Curriculum AlignmentThis resource supports achievement across multiple curriculum areas, particularly Digital Technologies and Key Competencies.
