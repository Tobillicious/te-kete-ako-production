    Literacy Essentials

    Key Skills Checklist

    • Read and understand a range of texts (minimum 2000 words)
    • Write clear, structured responses (minimum 500 words)
    • Use appropriate vocabulary and terminology
    • Demonstrate critical thinking in responses
    • Apply proofreading strategies

    Common Literacy Standards

    Standard Description Credits
    US 32405 Write formal texts for academic purposes 5
    US 32406 Read written texts to understand ideas and information 5

    Numeracy Essentials

    Key Skills Checklist

    • Solve problems using number operations
    • Interpret statistical information
    • Apply measurement concepts
    • Use geometric reasoning
    • Demonstrate algebraic thinking

    Common Numeracy Standards

    Standard Description Credits
    US 32401 Apply numeric reasoning in solving problems 5
    US 32402 Interpret statistical information for a purpose 5
