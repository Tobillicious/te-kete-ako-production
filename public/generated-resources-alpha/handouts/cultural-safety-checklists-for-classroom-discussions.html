Cultural Context: Tikanga Considerations

Cultural safety (ahurea haumaru) is grounded in MƒÅori concepts of:

  • Manaakitanga - Showing respect, hospitality and care for others
  • Whanaungatanga - Building and maintaining relationships
  • Wairuatanga - Recognizing spiritual dimensions of learning
  • Kotahitanga - Unity and collective responsibility

These principles should guide all classroom interactions, particularly when discussing sensitive topics or MƒÅori perspectives.
