Traditional MƒÅori knowledge systems developed over centuries of observation and adaptation to Aotearoa's unique environment. This mƒÅtauranga (knowledge) includes sophisticated understanding of energy flows, thermal dynamics, and sustainable resource use.

  • Invite local kaumƒÅtua (elders) or experts to contribute where possible

Modern Applications

Contemporary projects integrating traditional knowledge:

  • Geothermal power generation informed by traditional hot spring uses
  • Biomass energy systems based on traditional fuel knowledge
  • Micro-hydro systems inspired by traditional water management

Design Challenge

Create a sustainable energy solution for your school that combines traditional knowledge with modern technology:

  1. Identify an energy need (heating, lighting, etc.)
  2. Research traditional solutions
  3. Investigate modern technologies
  4. Design a hybrid solution
  5. Present your proposal with scientific justification
