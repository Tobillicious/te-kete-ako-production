Understanding MÄori Data Sovereignty

He WhakamÄrama | The Explanation

MÄori Data Sovereignty asserts that MÄori have the right to control, access, and manage MÄori data and information. This includes:

  • Rangatiratanga: Self-determination over data
  • Whakapapa: Understanding data lineage and connections
  • Kaitiakitanga: Guardianship and protection of data
  • Manaakitanga: Ethical and respectful data use

Interactive Scenario: The AI Health App

Imagine an AI health app being developed using health data from Aotearoa communities.

Question 1: How might this app impact MÄori data sovereignty?

Key principles: Free, prior and informed consent; MÄori leadership in development; equitable benefit sharing; cultural safety in algorithms.
