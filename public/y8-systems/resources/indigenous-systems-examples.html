    Resource 5.1C: Indigenous Systems Examples

    Hauora (Health System)

    Purpose: Wellness of whānau, hapū, and iwi through holistic care.

    Key Features: Spiritual, mental, physical, and family health treated together. Rongoā (traditional medicine) alongside modern healthcare.

    Rūnanga (Council System)

    Purpose: Community decision-making and conflict resolution.

    Key Features: Consensus-based decisions, elder wisdom, collective responsibility for outcomes.

    Kaitiakitanga (Environmental Management)

    Purpose: Sustainable guardianship of natural resources.

    Key Features: Seven-generation thinking, spiritual connection to nature, sustainable harvesting practices.

    Whakapapa (Genealogical System)

    Purpose: Connect people to each other, land, and knowledge.

    Key Features: Tracks relationships, inheritance rights, cultural knowledge, and responsibilities.

    Hui (Gathering System)

    Purpose: Community consultation and collective decision-making.

    Key Features: Everyone has voice, consensus seeking, shared responsibility for implementation.
