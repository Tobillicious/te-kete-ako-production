Refund Policy

Fair, transparent refund terms for all subscribers

Effective Date: October 26, 2025

✅ 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind our platform. If you're not satisfied within 14 days of your first payment, we'll refund you in full—no questions asked!

1. Free Trial Period

Individual Plans: 14-day free trial
School Plans: 30-day free trial

  • No credit card required to start your free trial
  • Cancel anytime during the trial period for no charge
  • Full access to all features during trial
  • Email reminder 7 days before trial ends

2. Money-Back Guarantee

Individual Monthly Plan ($15/month)

  • First Payment: Full refund if requested within 14 days
  • Renewal Payments: No refunds (you can cancel anytime for future billing)
  • How to Request: Email refunds@tekete.co.nz within 14 days

Individual Annual Plan (~$150/year)

  • First Payment: Full refund if requested within 14 days
  • After 14 Days: Pro-rated refund for unused months (minus one month processing fee)
  • Renewal Payments: Full refund if requested within 14 days

School Annual Plan ($499/year)

  • First Payment: Full refund if requested within 30 days
  • After 30 Days: Pro-rated refund for unused months (minus one month processing fee)
  • Minimum Usage: Schools must use platform for at least 2 months before refund eligibility

Enterprise Plans

Enterprise refund terms are specified in your custom agreement. Please refer to your contract or contact your account manager.

3. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund:

  1. Email refunds@tekete.co.nz with:
    • Your account email address
    • Reason for refund (optional but helpful for improving our service)
    • Date of payment
  2. We'll respond within 1-2 business days
  3. If approved, refund processed within 5-10 business days
  4. Refund appears on your original payment method

Processing Time:

  • Approval: 1-2 business days
  • Processing: 5-10 business days to your account
  • Bank Processing: May take additional 3-5 days depending on your bank

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • After the applicable refund period has passed
  • For violation of Terms of Service leading to account termination
  • For "change of mind" after using significant platform resources
  • For technical issues resolved within the refund period
  • For promotional or discounted subscriptions (special terms apply)

5. Cancellation vs Refund

Important Distinction:

  • Cancellation: Stops future billing, keeps current access
  • Refund: Returns money, may end access immediately

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Account Settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, and you retain access until then—no refund needed!

6. Partial Refunds

For annual plans cancelled mid-term:

  • Refund calculated as: (Unused months ÷ 12) × Annual price - Processing fee
  • Processing fee: One month's worth of subscription value
  • Example: $150 annual, 6 months used → ($150 ÷ 12 × 6) - $12.50 = ~$62.50 refund

7. Promotional Credits & Discounts

  • Promotional credits are non-refundable
  • Discounted subscriptions may have different refund terms (stated at purchase)
  • Referral credits cannot be converted to cash refunds

8. School/District Special Cases

Schools with extenuating circumstances may be eligible for special consideration:

  • School closure or merger
  • Budget cuts or funding changes
  • Significant change in student enrollment
  • Natural disasters or emergencies

Contact schools@tekete.co.nz to discuss options.

9. Disputes

If you're not satisfied with our refund decision:

  1. Reply to our refund decision email with additional information
  2. Request escalation to our Customer Care team
  3. We'll review within 5 business days and provide final decision

All refund decisions are subject to New Zealand consumer protection laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date". Material changes will be communicated via email to active subscribers.
