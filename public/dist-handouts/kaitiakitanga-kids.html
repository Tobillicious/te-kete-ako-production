Kaitiakitanga Kids

A hands-on activity guide for young environmental guardians

🌱 Learning Objectives

By completing this handout, students will:

  • Understand the concept of kaitiakitanga (guardianship)
  • Identify ways to care for their local environment
  • Create a personal action plan for environmental protection
  • Connect Māori environmental values with modern conservation
