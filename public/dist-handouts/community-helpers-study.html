Community Helpers Study

Discover the people who help our communities thrive

🤝 Learning Objectives

Through this study, students will:

  • Identify different community helpers and their roles
  • Understand the concept of manaakitanga (caring for others)
  • Recognize how community helpers support collective wellbeing
  • Connect community service to cultural values
