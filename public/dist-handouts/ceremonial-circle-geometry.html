Ceremonial Circle Geometry

Explore the mathematics of circular gathering spaces

⭕ Learning Objectives

Through exploring ceremonial circles, students will:

  • Understand properties of circles (radius, diameter, circumference)
  • Calculate measurements for circular spaces
  • Appreciate the cultural significance of circular gathering spaces
  • Apply geometric thinking to traditional practices

📐 Part 1: Circle Properties

Key parts of a circle:

📍 Center Point

The middle of the circle (te pū)

📏 Radius

Distance from center to edge

↔️ Diameter

Distance across (2 × radius)

⭕ Circumference

Distance around (π × diameter)

Cultural Connection: Circular spaces (like the marae ātea) create equality - everyone is the same distance from the center, no one is at the "head" or "back".
