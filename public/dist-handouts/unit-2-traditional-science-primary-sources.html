The Unwritten Encyclopedia

Before written language was introduced to Aotearoa, MƒÅori held a vast encyclopedia of scientific knowledge in their minds. This knowledge, or mƒÅtauranga, was passed down through generations in k≈çrero (stories), waiata (songs), and whakapapa (genealogies).
