Waka Construction Geometry

Explore the mathematical precision of traditional waka building

🛶 Learning Objectives

Through this exploration of waka construction, students will:

  • Apply geometric concepts to traditional waka design
  • Understand symmetry, proportion, and balance in boat building
  • Calculate angles, measurements, and ratios used in construction
  • Connect mathematical thinking with traditional Māori engineering

∠ Angles
