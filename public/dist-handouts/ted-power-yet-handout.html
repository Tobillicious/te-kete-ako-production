🧠 Fixed vs Growth Mindset

❌ Fixed Mindset ✅ Growth Mindset ("Yet")
"I can't do this" "I can't do this YET"
"I'm not good at math" "I'm learning how to do this better"
"I give up" "I'll try a different strategy"
"This is too hard" "This will take time and effort"

💭 Reflection Questions

  1. Self-Assessment: Think of a time you had a fixed mindset. What were you thinking? How did it affect your learning?
  2. The Power of "Yet": How does adding "yet" to "I can't do this" change the meaning and your feelings?
  3. Brain Science: Carol Dweck talks about the brain growing with effort. How does this change your view of mistakes and challenges?
  4. Cultural Connection: How does growth mindset connect to the concept of ako (reciprocal learning) in Māori culture?
