🌿 Te Reo as Living Language

We don't teach te reo Māori as a separate subject. Instead, te reo is woven throughout all learning — in science, mathematics, English, social studies, and digital technologies. This reflects how language is actually used: in authentic, meaningful contexts.

📐 Mathematics

Geometry with kōwhaiwhai patterns, statistics through maramataka, algebra in traditional games

🔬 Science

Traditional medicine (rongoa), lunar calendar science, ecology through kaitiakitanga

📝 English

Pūrākau (traditional narratives), whakataukī analysis, bilingual creative writing

🌍 Social Studies

Treaty analysis, whakapapa genealogy, decolonization in te reo contexts

💻 Digital Tech

Digital kaitiakitanga vocabulary, tech terms in te reo, online communities

🎨 All Subjects

Greetings, cultural protocols, subject-specific vocabulary, authentic communication

📚 Our Language Acquisition Approach

✅ We DO

  • Integrate te reo in authentic contexts
  • Use subject-specific vocabulary
  • Model correct pronunciation
  • Celebrate mistakes as learning
  • Connect to cultural frameworks
  • Make language functional & practical

❌ We DON'T

  • Teach grammar in isolation
  • Focus on rote memorization
  • Create artificial "language lessons"
  • Expect perfection immediately
  • Separate language from culture
  • Use decontextualized vocabulary lists
