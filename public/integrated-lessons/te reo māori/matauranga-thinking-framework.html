Understanding Through a Māori Worldview

🎯 Key Māori Concepts for Understanding

Whakapapa (Relationships)

Everything is connected through genealogy and relationships. Understanding connections reveals deeper meaning.

Tapu & Noa (Sacred & Common)

Understanding what is sacred (tapu) and what is everyday (noa) guides respectful behavior.

Mauri (Life Force)

Everything (rivers, forests, even rocks) has mauri - essential life force. Kaitiaki protect mauri.

Mana (Authority, Prestige)

Mana is earned through actions, knowledge, and service to community. It can be enhanced or diminished.
