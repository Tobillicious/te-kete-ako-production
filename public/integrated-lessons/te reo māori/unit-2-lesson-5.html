🎤 Mihimihi: Formal Introductions

Speaking with Confidence on the Marae Ātea

🌿 Mihimihi Structure

Traditional Order:

  1. Tēnā koutou katoa - Greetings to you all
  2. Pepeha - Your mountain, river, waka, iwi connection
  3. Ko wai ahau - Who you are (name, role)
  4. He aha tāku kaupapa - Your purpose for speaking
  5. Ngā mihi - Thanks and closing

Example Mihimihi:

Tēnā koutou, tēnā koutou, tēnā koutou katoa

Ko Taranaki te maunga

Ko Whanganui te awa

Ko Aotea te waka

Ko Ngāti Apa te iwi

Ko Aroha tōku ingoa

He kura tuarua ahau

Kia ora, kia ora, kia ora
