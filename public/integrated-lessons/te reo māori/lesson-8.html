Sentence Structure

Pattern: Kei te [verb] ahau = I am [verb]ing

Examples:

  • Kei te kai ahau = I am eating
  • Kei te ako ahau = I am learning
  • Kei te haere ahau = I am going

Practice Activity

Charades! Act out verbs and have classmates guess in te reo: "Kei te [verb] koe?"
