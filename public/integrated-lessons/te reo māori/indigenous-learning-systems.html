Tuakana-Teina Model

Peer Learning Partnership:

Tuakana (older/more experienced): Guides and supports teina

Teina (younger/less experienced): Learns and asks questions

Benefits of This Model:

  • Tuakana reinforces their knowledge by teaching
  • Teina gets personalized support
  • Builds whanaungatanga (relationships)
  • Everyone has a role and responsibility

Ako - Reciprocal Learning

In te ao MƒÅori, ako means both "to teach" AND "to learn"

This reflects the belief that:

  • Teachers learn from students too
  • Everyone has knowledge to share
  • Learning is a collaborative process
  • The expert/novice roles can shift

Practical Application

Implement Tuakana-Teina in Your Learning:

  1. Pair up with someone at a different skill level
  2. Tuakana teaches a concept to teina
  3. Teina asks questions and practices
  4. Roles can switch for different topics!
