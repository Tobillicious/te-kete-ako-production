💡 Economic inequality: Māori are 17% of population but hold only ~5% of national wealth. This reflects historical land confiscation and ongoing systemic barriers.

💪 Rangatiratanga: Economic Self-Determination

Despite historical injustice, Māori are building economic independence through:

🏢 Iwi-Led Businesses

Treaty settlements fund commercial ventures (forestry, fisheries, tourism)

🌾 Land Development

Returning to traditional agriculture and environmental management

🎓 Education & Skills

Increasing Māori participation in STEM, trades, entrepreneurship
