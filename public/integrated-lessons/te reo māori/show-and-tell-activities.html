Object Ideas

What to bring:

  • Family taonga (treasure)
  • Cultural item (koru necklace, woven bracelet)
  • Special book or toy
  • Photo of special place
  • Something you made
  • Gift from someone special

Preparation Steps

  1. Choose your object (something meaningful)
  2. Write your script (5-6 sentences in te reo)
  3. Practice pronunciation (use online dictionary)
  4. Get feedback (teacher/peer check)
  5. Practice delivery (speak clearly, make eye contact)
  6. Present with confidence!
