Reflection Prompts

Regular Reflection Questions:

  • What am I most proud of learning?
  • What challenged me? How did I overcome it?
  • How have I grown since the start of term?
  • What are my goals for next term?
  • How does this learning connect to my identity/culture?

Portfolio Organization

Suggested Structure:

  1. Cover Page: Name, year level, term/year
  2. Introduction: About me, my learning goals
  3. Subject Sections: Best work from each subject
  4. Reflections: Learning journal entries
  5. Achievements: Certificates, awards, feedback
  6. Goals: Future aspirations and plans

Digital vs Physical Kete

Digital Portfolio Options:

  • Google Sites
  • Seesaw
  • Padlet
  • Personal blog

Physical Portfolio:

  • Ring binder with dividers
  • Decorated folder
  • Scrapbook style

Choose the format that works best for you!
