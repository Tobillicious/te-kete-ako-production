Your First Steps in Learning the Language

🌿 Why Learn Te Reo Māori?

  • 🇳🇿 It's an official language of Aotearoa
  • 🧠 Connects you to NZ's Indigenous culture
  • ❤️ Shows respect for tangata whenua
  • 💼 Valuable skill for careers in NZ
  • 🌍 Preserves an endangered language

🔤 Te Pū - The Māori Alphabet

Vowels (Ngā Orokati):

ah

E

eh

I

ee

O

oh

U

oo

Consonants (Ngā Konohono):

H K M N P R T W NG WH

NG = like "ng" in "sing" | WH = like "f" in "fish"
