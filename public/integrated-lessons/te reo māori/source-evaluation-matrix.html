Source Evaluation Matrix: Critical Information Literacy

Evaluating Information Sources

The CRAAP Test:

CriterionQuestions to Ask
Currency (Timeliness) When was it published? Is it up-to-date?
Relevance (Importance) Does it answer your question? Who is the audience?
Authority (Source) Who is the author? What are their credentials?
Accuracy (Reliability) Is it supported by evidence? Can you verify it?
Purpose (Reason) Why was it created? Is there bias?
