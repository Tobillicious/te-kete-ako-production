Comprehensive Review Activity

Create a Te Reo Conversation

With a partner, create a conversation that includes:

  1. Greetings
  2. Introductions (name, where you're from)
  3. Asking how someone is feeling
  4. Describing something (using colors/numbers)
  5. Farewells

Present your conversation to the class!
