Possessives in Te Reo Māori

Learning Objectives

  • Use possessive words correctly
  • Distinguish between tōku/tāku categories
  • Describe ownership and relationships

Two Types of Possessives

Tō Category (You control/dominate):

  • Tōku whare = My house
  • Tōku tuahine = My sister
  • Things you can control or have authority over

Tā Category (You don't control):

  • Tāku ingoa = My name
  • Tāku māmā = My mother
  • Things that control you or you don't dominate
