👋 Ngā Ohaaki: Greetings & Farewells

💬 Essential Greetings

Kia ora

Hello, hi, thanks (most common greeting)

Tēnā koe (to 1), Tēnā kōrua (to 2), Tēnā koutou (to 3+)

Formal greetings - number depends on how many people

Mōrena

Good morning

Kia ora tātou katoa

Hello everyone (inclusive - includes speaker)

👋 Farewells

Ka kite

See you later, goodbye (casual)

Haere rā

Farewell (said to person leaving)

E noho rā

Farewell (said to person staying)

Hei konā / Hei konei

See you later (informal)

🎭 Role Play Practice

Scenario: Meeting Your Teacher

Practice this dialogue with a partner:

You: Mōrena whaea!

Teacher: Mōrena! Kei te pēhea koe?

You: Kei te pai ahau, ngā mihi. Kei te pēhea koe?

Teacher: Kei te tino pai! Ka kite āpōpō.

You: Āe, ka kite whaea!
