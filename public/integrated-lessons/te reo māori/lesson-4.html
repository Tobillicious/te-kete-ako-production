👥 Whānau Vocabulary

Whānau

Family, extended family

Matua / Pāpā

Father, dad

Whaea / Māmā

Mother, mum

Tuakana

Older sibling (same gender)

Teina

Younger sibling (same gender)

Kaumātua

Elders, grandparents
