Pepeha - Your Personal Introduction

"Ko te tangata, he maunga"

"A person is like a mountain" - We all have our landmarks

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the concept and purpose of pepeha
  • Create your own pepeha
  • Present your pepeha with confidence
  • Appreciate connections to place and identity

What is a Pepeha?

A pepeha is a way of introducing yourself by connecting to:

  • Your river (awa)

Create Your Pepeha

Steps:

  1. Research local geographic features (mountains, rivers, harbors)
  2. Choose places meaningful to you (birthplace, where you live, ancestral homelands)
  3. Write out your pepeha using the template
  4. Practice pronunciation
  5. Share with the class!

Why Pepeha Matter

Pepeha reflect MƒÅori worldview:

  • Connection to land: We're part of the environment, not separate
  • Whakapapa: Genealogical connections to place
  • Identity: Where we're from shapes who we are
  • Respect: Acknowledging the land that sustains us

Assessment

  • Create your personal pepeha (written)
  • Present your pepeha to the class (oral)
  • Design a visual representation of your pepeha
  • Write a reflection: Why are these places important to you?
