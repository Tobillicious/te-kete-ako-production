📚 Learning Area Connections

English / Te Reo Māori:

  • Listening, Reading, Viewing: Comprehension across media types
  • Speaking, Writing, Presenting: Creating coherent texts
  • Cultural Contexts: Understanding diverse perspectives

Mathematics & Statistics:

  • Number: Operations, patterns, relationships
  • Geometry: Shapes, measurement, spatial reasoning
  • Statistics: Data investigation, probability
  • Cultural integration: Māori patterns, navigation, counting systems

Science:

  • Nature of Science: Inquiry, investigation
  • Living World: Ecology, evolution, biodiversity
  • Planet Earth & Beyond: Earth systems, astronomy
  • Mātauranga Māori: Traditional ecological knowledge

Social Sciences:

  • Identity, Culture, Organisation: Understanding society
  • Place & Environment: Geographic understanding
  • Continuity & Change: Historical thinking
  • Economic World: Resources and systems

Digital Technologies:

  • Computational Thinking: Algorithms, decomposition
  • Designing & Developing: Creating digital outcomes
  • Data sovereignty: Māori data governance

🌿 Te Marautanga o Aotearoa (Māori-Medium Curriculum)

For kura kaupapa Māori and Māori-medium education, resources align with te marautanga principles:

  • Te Ira Tangata: Human essence and identity
  • Te Ao: The world and environment
  • Te Reo: Language as living treasure
  • Ngā Tikanga: Cultural practices and protocols
