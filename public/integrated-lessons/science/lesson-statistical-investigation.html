üìè Mean (Average)

Add all values, divide by how many

Mean = Sum √∑ Count

Example: (5+7+3+8+2) √∑ 5 = 5

üéØ Median (Middle Value)

Line up values in order, find the middle

2, 3, 5, 7, 8

Median = 5 (middle value)

üîù Mode (Most Common)

The value that appears most often

Mode = 5 (appears 3 times)

‚ÜîÔ∏è Range (Spread)
