    📚 Introduction

    Artificial Intelligence systems are increasingly used to process and analyze data about communities, including Māori communities. This lesson explores the ethical responsibilities around AI and data through the framework of Māori data sovereignty.

    Key Concepts:

    • Data Sovereignty: The right of communities to control their own data
    • Rangatiratanga: Self-determination and authority
    • Kaitiakitanga: Guardianship and protection of knowledge
    • Whakapapa: Connections and relationships in data
