üìù Practice: Analyze This Headline

"Shocking Discovery: Scientists Say This One Food Kills Cancer Cells!"

Apply the 5 Questions:

üö© Red Flags in This Headline:

  • Emotional language: "Shocking" creates urgency/fear
  • Vague claims: "This one food" - what food? Where's the detail?
  • Overpromising: "Kills cancer cells" is a huge medical claim
  • Authority appeal: "Scientists say" - which scientists? What study?
  • Purpose: Likely clickbait to generate ad revenue, not inform
