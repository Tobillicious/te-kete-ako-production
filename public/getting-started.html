🚀 Getting Started

Your quick-start guide to Te Kete Ako

⏱️ Takes just 3 minutes to get started!

👨‍🏫 For Teachers

1 Browse by Subject

Click on your subject in the navigation menu (Science, Mathematics, English, Social Studies, etc.) to see all available resources for that subject.

💡 Pro Tip: Subject hubs show resources organized by year level and unit. Great for planning a full term!
2 Find a Resource

Use the search bar on the homepage or browse by year level. Each resource shows:

  • Quality score (aim for 85+!)
  • Cultural integration status (🌿 icon)
  • Year level and subject
  • Whether it has handouts, assessments, or games
3 Use in Your Class

Click on a lesson to open it. You can:

  • Print: Press Ctrl+P or Cmd+P - automatically formatted for printing
  • Project: Display the lesson on your classroom screen
  • Share Link: Copy the URL and send to students
  • Adapt: Use as-is or modify for your class needs
💡 Pro Tip: Most lessons have companion handouts and assessments. Look for the "Resources" section on the lesson page!

📚 For Students

1 Find Your Learning Resource

Your teacher will give you a link, OR you can search for the topic on the homepage. Type what you're learning about (e.g., "Year 9 ecosystems") and hit enter!

2 Work Through the Lesson

Read the content, watch any videos, complete activities. Take your time - learning is a journey, not a race! ("Tāria te kōrero, kia puta te whakaaro" - Pause to reflect, so wisdom may emerge)

3 Complete Assessment (if assigned)

Many lessons have assessments or reflection activities. Complete these to show your learning. Some track your progress automatically if you're logged in!
