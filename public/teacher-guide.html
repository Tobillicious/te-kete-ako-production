    📊 Dashboard Features Overview

    🎯 Curriculum Coverage Analysis

    Track student engagement with specific NZ Curriculum Achievement Objectives. See which learning areas need more attention and celebrate progress toward curriculum goals.

    📈 Learning Progression Trends

    Visualize how students navigate through your educational content. Identify effective learning pathways and optimize resource sequencing.

    🧠 Content Effectiveness Metrics

    Discover which resources are most engaging and effective. Get data-driven insights on time spent, interaction depth, and learning progression.

    🌟 Cultural Engagement Tracking

    Monitor student interaction with Te Ao Māori content and cultural learning elements. Ensure authentic cultural integration across all subjects.

    🤖 AI-Powered Insights

    Learning Style Analysis

    The system automatically analyzes student behavior patterns to identify learning preferences:

    • Visual/Reading Learners: Prefer handouts and written materials
    • Kinesthetic/Interactive Learners: Thrive with games and hands-on activities
    • Auditory/Visual Learners: Benefit from video content and multimedia
    • Multimodal Learners: Engage well with diverse approaches

    Predictive Recommendations

    Get personalized suggestions for:

    • Next learning activities based on current progress
    • Curriculum gaps that need attention
    • Cultural integration opportunities
    • Teaching strategy adaptations
    🌟 Cultural Intelligence: The system includes specific recommendations for integrating Te Ao Māori perspectives and mātauranga Māori approaches.

    📋 Generating Reports

    Comprehensive Data Export

    Click "Export Comprehensive Report" to download detailed JSON data including:

    • Curriculum engagement metrics
    • Content effectiveness scores
    • Learning pathway analysis
    • Predictive insights and recommendations

    Teacher-Friendly Insights Report

    Click "Generate Class Insights" for a formatted report with:

    • Learning style analysis and teaching adaptations
    • Curriculum focus areas needing attention
    • Recommended next steps for students
    • Cultural integration opportunities
    💼 School Leadership: These reports are perfect for sharing with senior leadership, curriculum coordinators, and during appraisal discussions.

    🎮 Real-Time Features

    Achievement Notifications

    Students receive motivating notifications when they reach learning milestones:

    • 🎯 Curriculum Explorer (10+ Achievement Objectives)
    • 🌟 Learning Pathfinder (20+ pages explored)
    • More achievements unlock automatically!

    Adaptive Learning Suggestions

    Personalized content recommendations appear based on individual learning patterns. Students get suggestions perfectly matched to their learning style and progress.

    🔧 Technical Integration

    Seamless Operation

    The analytics system works automatically across all Te Kete Ako resources:

    • Handouts: Track reading time, scroll depth, and engagement
    • Games: Monitor gameplay, score progression, and cultural bonus points
    • Lessons: Analyze activity completion and assessment interactions
    • Videos: Track YouTube resource access and educational content viewing

    Privacy & Data Security

    All analytics data is stored locally in the browser. No personal information is transmitted or stored externally. Teachers maintain complete control over their classroom data.

    🛡️ Privacy First: This system respects student privacy while providing valuable educational insights. Perfect for schools with strict data protection requirements.
