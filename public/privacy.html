Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

Te Kete Ako is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020.

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information, with special consideration for students under 13 and educational data.

1. Information We Collect

Student Accounts:

  • Name (first name, last name)
  • Email address (optional for under-13s)
  • School name and year level
  • Cultural background (optional, for personalized content)
  • Learning preferences
  • Game scores and progress (anonymous)

Teacher Accounts:

  • Full name and professional title
  • Email address
  • School name and registration number
  • Teaching subjects and year levels
  • KAMAR integration data (if opted in)
  • Class lists and student enrollment

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Technical errors (for debugging)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve educational services
  • Personalize learning content based on year level and preferences
  • Enable teachers to manage classes and track student progress
  • Communicate important platform updates
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations under New Zealand law

3. Data Protection for Students Under 13

Parental Consent: For students under 13, we require verifiable parental/guardian consent before creating an account.

Limited Data Collection: We collect only the minimum information necessary for educational purposes.

No Marketing: We never use student data for marketing purposes or share it with advertisers.

Deletion Rights: Parents can request deletion of their child's account and data at any time.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Te Kete Ako does NOT sell personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

With Educational Institutions:

  • Teachers can access their own students' progress
  • Schools can access aggregated (anonymous) class data

Service Providers:

  • Hosting providers (Supabase, Netlify) - data stored in New Zealand/Australia
  • Authentication services (Supabase Auth)
  • All providers comply with NZ Privacy Act 2020

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by New Zealand law
  • To protect safety or rights of users
  • In response to valid legal processes

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data backups and disaster recovery

6. Data Retention

Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active and for educational purposes.

Inactive Accounts: After 2 years of inactivity, accounts may be archived or deleted.

Deletion Requests: Users can request account deletion at any time. Data will be deleted within 30 days, except where legal retention is required.

7. Your Privacy Rights (Privacy Act 2020)

Under New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Object: Object to certain data processing
  • Complain: Lodge a complaint with the Privacy Commissioner

8. Cookies and Tracking

Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality (login, preferences)

Analytics: We use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to improve the platform. No personally identifiable information is tracked.

No Third-Party Advertising: We do not use advertising cookies or trackers.

9. Cultural Data

Information about cultural background and iwi affiliation (if provided) is:

  • Voluntary and optional
  • Used only to personalize cultural content
  • Handled with cultural sensitivity and respect
  • Never shared without explicit consent

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes via email or platform announcement.
