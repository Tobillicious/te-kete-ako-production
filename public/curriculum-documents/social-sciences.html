🌍 Achievement Objectives - Level 4 (Years 7-8)

🏛️ Identity, Culture, and Organisation

Y7 Y8

Understanding Society

  • Understand how people make decisions about access to and use of resources
  • Understand how people participate individually and collectively in response to community challenges
  • Understand how different groups contribute to society
  • Explore tikanga Māori approaches to community decision-making and resource stewardship

🗺️ Place and Environment

Geographic Understanding

  • Understand how people's perception of and interaction with natural and cultural environments differs
  • Understand how people view and use places differently
  • Explore how people respond to environmental and social changes
  • Connect to kaitiakitanga (environmental guardianship) and whenua (land relationships)

🔍 Social Inquiry Process

Students develop critical thinking through a structured inquiry process:

1️⃣ Ask Questions

Pose social inquiry questions

2️⃣ Gather & Organize

Collect and sort information

3️⃣ Analyze & Interpret

Evaluate and interpret sources

4️⃣ Communicate

Share findings and conclusions

🌿 Tikanga Māori in Social Sciences

Te Kete Ako uniquely centers tikanga Māori and Te Ao Māori worldviews in social sciences learning:

🏛️ Whakapapa - Interconnection

Understanding genealogical connections and social relationships

🤝 Manaakitanga - Care

Hospitality, generosity, and community support

⚖️ Tika - Justice

Fairness, rights, and restorative justice
