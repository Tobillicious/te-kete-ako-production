🔬Achievement Objectives - Level 4 (Years 7-8)

🌿 Nature of Science

Y7Y8

Understanding About Science

  • Appreciate that scientists ask questions about our world that lead to investigations
  • Understand that science knowledge evolves over time
  • Recognize that science is a way of explaining the world
  • Connect western science with mātauranga Māori approaches to observation and understanding

Investigating in Science

  • Plan and conduct scientific investigations
  • Use a variety of methods to collect and interpret data
  • Use evidence to develop explanations of events and phenomena
  • Communicate findings in a variety of ways

🦎 Living World

Life Processes

  • Recognize that there are life processes common to all living things
  • Understand how organisms are suited to their particular habitat

Ecology

  • Explain how living things are suited to their particular habitat
  • Investigate the relationship between organisms and their environment
  • Connect to kaitiakitanga (guardianship) and traditional ecological knowledge
