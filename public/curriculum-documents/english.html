📖Achievement Objectives - Level 4 (Years 7-8)

📚 Listening, Reading, and Viewing

Y7Y8

Purposes and Audiences

  • Show a developing understanding of how texts are shaped for different purposes and audiences
  • Identify particular points of view and differences in viewpoints
  • Evaluate the reliability of texts

Ideas

  • Show a developing understanding of ideas within, across, and beyond texts
  • Identify and evaluate the ways language features are used in texts
  • Make connections between texts

✍️ Speaking, Writing, and Presenting

Purposes and Audiences

  • Construct texts that show a developing awareness of purpose and audience
  • Select and use language features appropriately
  • Adapt speaking, writing, and presenting for different contexts

Ideas and Structure

  • Develop ideas clearly with supporting details
  • Organize texts coherently using appropriate structures
  • Use a variety of sentence structures effectively
