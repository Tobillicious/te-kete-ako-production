🎨 Achievement Objectives - Level 4 (Years 7-8)

📚 Understanding the Arts in Context

Y7 Y8

Cultural & Historical Perspectives

  • Explore and describe the contexts of arts from different times and places
  • Identify and describe how ideas and feelings are communicated in arts works
  • Connect to Māori art forms: toi whakairo (carving), raranga (weaving), kapa haka (performing arts)

🛠️ Developing Practical Knowledge

Skills & Techniques

  • Explore and use elements and selected principles of arts disciplines
  • Develop specific skills and techniques in chosen art forms
  • Apply conventions and technologies associated with arts disciplines

🌿 Ngā Toi Māori - Māori Arts

Te Kete Ako celebrates and integrates traditional Māori art forms as foundational to our arts curriculum:

🗿 Whakairo - Carving

Traditional wood and stone carving with symbolic meanings

🧶 Raranga - Weaving

Flax weaving creating functional and artistic works

🎭 Kapa Haka - Performing Arts

Haka, waiata, poi - storytelling through performance

🎨 Tā Moko - Traditional Tattoo

Sacred body art telling stories of whakapapa

🖼️ Visual Arts

Painting, drawing, printmaking, sculpture, digital art
