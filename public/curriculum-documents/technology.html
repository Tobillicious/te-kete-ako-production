🔧 Achievement Objectives - Level 4 (Years 7-8)

⚙️ Technological Practice

Y7 Y8

Planning for Practice

  • Describe the nature of intended outcomes, explaining how they address the need or opportunity
  • Describe the key attributes that enable development and evaluation of an outcome
  • Describe how their outcomes address the identified need or opportunity

Brief Development

  • Describe the physical and functional nature of the outcome
  • Explain key considerations that inform the development
  • Justify specifications in terms of stakeholder feedback and wider community considerations
  • Consider traditional Māori approaches to problem-solving and community consultation

Outcome Development and Evaluation

  • Investigate a context to communicate potential outcomes
  • Evaluate these against attributes to select the outcome to develop
  • Test and evaluate the outcome against specifications

📚 Technological Knowledge

Technological Modeling

  • Understand that functional models are used to represent reality and test design concepts
  • Explore different types of models (physical, graphic, mathematical)

Technological Products

  • Understand that technological products are functional for a purpose
  • Understand the relationship between materials and properties

Technological Systems

  • Understand that technological systems have inputs, controlled transformations, and outputs
  • Understand how subsystems are interconnected
  • Connect to holistic systems thinking in te ao Māori

💡 Nature of Technology

Students explore how technology is influenced by and influences society:

  • Understand that technology both reflects and shapes society and culture
  • Recognize that technological outcomes may have positive and negative impacts
  • Understand intellectual property and ethical considerations
  • Explore Māori perspectives on data sovereignty and cultural intellectual property
