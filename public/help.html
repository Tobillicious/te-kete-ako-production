Q: The page isn't loading properly. What should I do?
Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge all supported)

Q: Can I use this offline?
Yes! Te Kete Ako is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Once you've visited a page, it will be available offline. On Chrome/Edge, you can also "Install" the app from your browser menu for offline access.
Q: Is my data safe and private?
Yes. We use industry-standard security (Supabase authentication) and follow New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 requirements. We never sell your data. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
