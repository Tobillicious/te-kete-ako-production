🚀 10-Point Revolutionary Plan

Grounded in GraphRAG evidence, ready for implementation

  • 1. Kaupapa Framework
    Replace subjects with 12 holistic themes, accessible Y1-13 at different depths
  • 2. Te Reo as Foundation
    Embed Māori language in all kaupapa, not as separate subject
  • 3. Wānanga Networks
    Mixed-age learning spaces, tuakana-teina pedagogy, collaborative knowledge construction
  • 4. Teacher Professionalism
    13,042 variants prove expertise diversity - provide choice, not mandates
  • 5. Assessment as Navigation
    Portfolio learning journeys, not grades. GraphRAG maps pathways forward.
  • 6. AI as Tuakana
    Technology suggests and enables, never replaces human relationships
  • 7. Cultural Infrastructure
    Target 50% whakataukī integration by 2026 (currently 27.5%)
  • 8. Evidence-Based Evolution
    GraphRAG tracks patterns, platform learns from usage, continuous improvement
  • 9. Open Networks
    Share frameworks freely, enable schools to adapt, build learning community
  • 10. New Success Metrics
    Network density, cultural depth, peer teaching, student agency - not just test scores
