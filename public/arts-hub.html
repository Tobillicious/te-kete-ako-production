🌿 Māori Artistic Traditions

Kowhaiwhai Patterns

Traditional rafter patterns combining mathematics, spirituality, and visual beauty. Each design tells a story and connects to whakapapa.

Whakairo (Carving)

Traditional wood carving techniques, symbolism in design, and the spiritual significance of carved taonga and wharenui decorations.

Kapa Haka

Traditional and contemporary Māori performing arts - haka, waiata, poi, and more. Performance as cultural expression and identity.
