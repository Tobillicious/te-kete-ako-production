🎯 Sample Integration Activities

📰 Media Analysis: Climate Change Coverage

English Component: Analyze media articles for bias, rhetorical strategies, and representation of Māori environmental perspectives

Science Component: Evaluate scientific accuracy of claims, interpret climate data, understand greenhouse effect

🌿 Cultural Integration: Examine how media portrays Māori kaitiakitanga and indigenous climate leadership

🧬 Debate: Genetic Technology Ethics

English Component: Argumentative writing, persuasive speaking, structured debate using Māori oratory traditions

Science Component: Understand CRISPR, genetic modification, DNA sequencing technology and applications

🌿 Cultural Integration: Apply Māori ethical frameworks around whakapapa, tapu, and collective guardianship

📖 Research Project: Traditional Ecological Knowledge

English Component: Interview skills, narrative writing, oral history documentation, research methods

Science Component: Document traditional plant knowledge, ecological observations, environmental monitoring practices

🌿 Cultural Integration: Value mātauranga Māori as legitimate science, practice respectful research with cultural protocols
