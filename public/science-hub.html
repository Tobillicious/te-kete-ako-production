🌍 Dual Knowledge Systems

Mātauranga Māori

  • Rongoa (traditional medicine)
  • Maramataka (lunar calendar)
  • Kaitiakitanga (environmental guardianship)
  • Whakapapa (interconnectedness)
  • Tohu (natural indicators)

Western Science

  • Scientific method
  • Experimentation & inquiry
  • Evidence-based reasoning
  • Systematic classification
  • Quantitative analysis

🌟 Both knowledge systems are equally valid and offer complementary perspectives on understanding the natural world.
