    ğŸ” Analysis Activities

    Activity 1: Purpose & Audience

    1. What is the author's primary purpose? (Persuade/Inform/Entertain)
    2. Who is the target audience? Consider age, role, interests
    3. What does the author want readers to do? Be specific
    4. How does the author establish credibility? (Ethos)

    Activity 2: Persuasive Techniques

    1. Find 3 examples of emotional appeals (Pathos)
    2. Identify logical arguments (Logos) - statistics, facts
    3. Spot rhetorical questions - underline them
    4. Find examples of repetition for emphasis

    Activity 3: Cultural Analysis

    2. What cultural perspectives might be missing?
