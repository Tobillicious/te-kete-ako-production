    🔍 Literal Comprehension (Finding Information)

    1 What is the main topic of this reading?
    2 According to the text, what are the key points mentioned?
    3 Who or what is mentioned as important in this text?
    4 When does this take place?

    🧠 Inferential Comprehension (Reading Between the Lines)

    5 What can you infer about the author's purpose in writing this text?
    6 Based on the information in the text, what might happen next?
    7 What does the author imply about [specific topic]?
    8 How do you think [character/subject] felt about [situation]? Use evidence from the text.

    💭 Critical Thinking (Evaluating and Analyzing)

    9 Do you agree with the author's viewpoint? Why or why not?
    10 What are the strengths and weaknesses of the argument presented?
    11 How does this text connect to what you already know about [topic]?
    12 What questions do you still have after reading this text?

    📝 Vocabulary and Language

    13 What does the word "[vocabulary word]" mean in this context?
    14 Find a word in the text that means the same as "[synonym]".
    15 What type of text is this? (narrative, informational, persuasive, etc.)

    🚀 Application and Extension

    16 How could you apply the information from this text to your own life or community?
    17 What would you like to research further based on this reading?
    18 Create a summary of the main ideas in 3-4 sentences.
