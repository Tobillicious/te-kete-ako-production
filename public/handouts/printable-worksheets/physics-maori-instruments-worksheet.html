  • a) Shorter air column = higher frequency = higher pitch. Covering holes makes column longer = lower pitch
  • b) Blowing harder increases amplitude (volume) but doesn't significantly change the length/frequency relationship that determines pitch

Question 4 - Percussion Physics:

  • a) Larger object vibrates more slowly = lower frequency = lower pitch (inverse relationship between size and frequency)
  • b) Density, hardness, elasticity all affect tone quality. Harder/denser materials produce clearer, sharper sounds

Question 5 - Shell Trumpet:

  • a) Large amplitude (loud sound), smooth transmission through air, resonance in the shell amplifies sound
  • b) Spiral acts as acoustic chamber that amplifies and projects sound, creates resonance that sustains the note
