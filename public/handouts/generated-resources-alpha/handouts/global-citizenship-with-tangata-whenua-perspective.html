Understanding Global Citizenship

Global citizenship typically refers to recognizing our interconnectedness with people and environments worldwide. From a Tangata Whenua perspective, this connects deeply with concepts like:

  • Whakapapa - Our genealogical connections to all living things
  • Kaitiakitanga - Guardianship of our environment
  • Manaakitanga - Hospitality and care for others
  • Tino rangatiratanga - Self-determination
