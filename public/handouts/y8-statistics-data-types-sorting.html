    📊 Data Types Sorting Activity

    Week 1: Introduction to Statistical Investigations | Y8 Statistics

    Name: _________________ Date: ____________

    Whakataukī: "Mā te mōhio ka ora, mā te ora ka mōhio"

    Translation: "Through knowledge comes wellbeing, through wellbeing comes knowledge"

    🎯 Learning Objective

    Identify whether data is categorical (can be grouped into categories) or numerical (can be measured or counted).

    📝 Instructions

    For each data example below, decide if it is categorical or numerical, then explain your reasoning.

    🔍 Data Sorting Table

    Data Example Categorical or Numerical? Why?
    Favourite kai (food)
    Height in centimeters
    Number of people in whānau
    Iwi affiliation
    Age in years
    Favourite sport
    Time spent on homework (minutes)
    Eye color
    Number of siblings
    Type of transport to school
