    How to Play Te Reo Māori Wordle (6-Letter)

    Goal

    Guess the hidden 6-letter Te Reo Māori word in 6 tries or fewer.

    How to Play

    • Type a 6-letter word and press Enter
    • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change
    • You have 6 attempts to guess correctly

    Color Meanings

    T Green: Letter is correct and in the right position

    A Yellow: Letter is in the word but in wrong position

    E Gray: Letter is not in the word

    Te Reo Māori Tips

    • All letters are pronounced - no silent letters!
    • Vowels: A (ah), E (eh), I (ee), O (oh), U (oo)
    • Common patterns: NG, WH, combinations with vowels
    • Think of Māori words you already know
