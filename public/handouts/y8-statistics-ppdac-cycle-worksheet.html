    The PPDAC Cycle is a framework for conducting statistical investigations:

    • Problem - What question are you trying to answer?
    • Plan - How will you collect data?
    • Data - Collect your data
    • Analysis - Analyze and display your data
    • Conclusion - What did you find out?
