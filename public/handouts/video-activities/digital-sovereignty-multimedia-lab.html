    🔬 Hands-On Investigation Projects

    🕵️ AI Bias Detective

    Mission: Test different AI systems for cultural bias and representation.

    1. Choose 3 different AI tools (text, image, or voice)
    2. Design tests for cultural awareness and bias
    3. Document results with screenshots/examples
    4. Analyze patterns in AI responses
    5. Propose improvements based on Māori values

    📊 Data Sovereignty Audit

    Mission: Investigate how your personal data is being used by tech companies.

    1. Download your data from 2 major platforms (Google, Facebook, etc.)
    2. Analyze what information they've collected
    3. Research these companies' AI training practices
    4. Apply CARE principles to evaluate their data governance
    5. Design a culturally-appropriate data policy

    🏆 Advanced Challenge: Community AI Policy

    Working in groups, develop AI governance guidelines for your school or local marae. Consider cultural protocols, data protection, and community benefit.

    Policy Components:

    • Cultural consultation requirements
    • Data ownership and consent protocols
    • AI bias monitoring systems
    • Community benefit sharing
    • Elder and youth engagement processes

    Presentation Format:

    • Policy document (2-3 pages)
    • Community presentation (10 minutes)
    • Visual infographic of key principles
    • Implementation timeline
    • Success metrics and evaluation
