    Cultural Context

    Traditional MÄori knowledge systems developed over centuries of observation and adaptation to Aotearoa's unique environment. This mÄtauranga (knowledge) includes sophisticated understanding of energy flows, thermal dynamics, and sustainable resource use.

    Cultural Safety Considerations

    • Always acknowledge the source when sharing traditional knowledge
    • Recognize that some knowledge may be tapu (sacred/restricted)
    • Invite local kaumÄtua (elders) or experts to contribute where possible
    • Present information as contemporary applications of traditional principles rather than "ancient" knowledge
    • Use te reo MÄori terms appropriately and consistently
