    Te Whakatupu Taonga | Resource Management

    Mathematical models can help determine sustainable harvesting rates for resources like fish (ika) or plants (rongoÄ).

    The Schaefer model for fisheries management:

    \[ H = qE X \]

    \[ \frac{dX}{dt} = rX \left(1 - \frac{X}{K}\right) - H \]

    Where:

    • H = harvest rate
    • q = catchability coefficient
    • E = fishing effort
    • X = fish population
    • r = intrinsic growth rate
    • K = carrying capacity
