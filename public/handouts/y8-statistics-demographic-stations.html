    🔍 Census Investigation Station Cards

    Week 3: Demographic Analysis Stations | Y8 Statistics

    Cut out these cards for station rotation activity

    Station 1: Tangata Whenua Growth 🌺

    Focus: Māori population trends

    Your Tasks:

    1. Calculate absolute increase in Māori population from 2018 to 2023
    2. If the same rate continues, estimate Māori population in 2028
    3. Research: What factors might contribute to this growth?
    4. Discuss: Why is this data important for education planning?

    Record Your Findings:

    Station 2: Pasifika Communities 🌊

    Focus: Pacific Islander population trends

    Your Tasks:

    1. Calculate Pasifika population percentage point change
    2. Estimate actual number of Pasifika people in 2023
    3. Research: Which Pacific nations contribute most to NZ population?
    4. Discuss: How does this connect to NZ's Pacific partnerships?

    Record Your Findings:

    Station 3: Asian Communities 🎋

    Focus: Asian population trends

    Your Tasks:

    1. Why did Asian population have largest percentage point increase (+2.2pp)?
    2. Calculate Asian population in actual numbers for 2023
    3. Research: Which Asian countries contribute most to NZ immigration?
    4. Discuss: How does cultural diversity benefit New Zealand?

    Record Your Findings:

    Station 4: Changing Demographics 📊

    Focus: Overall population trends

    Your Tasks:

    1. Calculate total NZ population increase (2018 to 2023)
    2. What does it mean that European/Pākehā percentage decreased?
    3. Research: How is Auckland's diversity different from other regions?
    4. Discuss: What challenges and opportunities does diversity create?

    Record Your Findings:
