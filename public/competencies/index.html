"Mā te mōhio ki te ao, ka ora ai te tangata"

Through knowledge of the world, people will thrive

NZ Curriculum Key Competencies

🧠

Critical Thinking

Questioning, analyzing, and evaluating information through whakaaro nui (deep reflection)

🤝

Collaboration

Working together through whānaungatanga - collective strength and shared success

🌿

Cultural Competence

Relating to others with manaakitanga and respect for Te Tiriti o Waitangi

💬

Communication

Expressing ideas through kōrero - language as cultural treasure and practical tool

💻

Digital Literacy

Using technology with kaitiakitanga - digital citizenship and data sovereignty

✨

Creativity & Innovation

Creating and innovating through auahatanga - cultural creativity meets modern design

🎯

Self-Management

Managing self through mauri (life force) - wellbeing, resilience, and personal growth
