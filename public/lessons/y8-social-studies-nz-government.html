YEAR 8 SOCIAL STUDIES

NZ Government & Māori Representation

🏛️ Social Studies (Civics) • ⏱️ 65 minutes • 👥 Year 8 • 🌿 Cultural: Māori seats & co-governance

🎯 WALT

  • Explain NZ's government system (MMP)
  • Understand Māori electoral seats
  • Identify co-governance models
  • Analyze representation & participation

✅ Success Criteria

  • I can explain how MMP works
  • I can explain Māori seats history
  • I can name 1 co-governance example
  • I can discuss representation issues

📚 Lesson Flow

1️⃣ How NZ Government Works (20 min)

MMP System:
  • 120 MPs total
  • 72 electorate MPs (including 7 Māori seats)
  • 48 list MPs (from party votes)
  • Government formed by party with most votes

2️⃣ Māori Electoral Seats (25 min)

History:
  • 1867: 4 Māori seats created (temporary - still here!)
  • Purpose: Ensure Māori representation
  • Only Māori can vote for Māori seats
  • Currently 7 Māori seats (based on Māori roll numbers)
  • Debate: Keep them or abolish? (political controversy)

3️⃣ Co-Governance Examples (20 min)

  • Te Urewera: No longer national park - self-governing legal entity (Tūhoe partnership)
  • Waikato River: Co-managed by Crown & iwi (equal say)
  • Health NZ: Māori Health Authority partnership
