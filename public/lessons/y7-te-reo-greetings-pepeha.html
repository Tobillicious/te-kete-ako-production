Te Reo Basics: Greetings & Pepeha

🌿 Te Reo Māori•⏱️ 60 min•👥 Year 7

🎯 WALT

  • Use basic greetings (kia ora, tēnā koe, etc.)
  • Understand pepeha structure
  • Create personal pepeha
  • Present pepeha confidently

✅ Success Criteria

  • I can greet in te reo correctly
  • I know my pepeha structure
  • I can say my pepeha
  • I understand what pepeha means

📚 Lesson

Common Greetings

  • Kia ora - Hello/Hi/Thank you
  • Tēnā koe - Hello (formal, to 1 person)
  • Tēnā kōrua - Hello (to 2 people)
  • Tēnā koutou - Hello (to 3+ people)
  • Haere mai - Welcome
  • Ka kite - Goodbye/See you
  • Haere rā - Goodbye (you're leaving)

Pepeha Structure

Pepeha = introduction connecting you to land, mountains, rivers, ancestors:

Ko [mountain] te maunga
Ko [river] te awa
Ko [iwi] te iwi
Ko [hapū] te hapū
Ko [marae] te marae
Ko [name] tōku ingoa
Nō [place] ahau
