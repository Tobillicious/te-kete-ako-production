AI Bias Examples (30 min)

Cases to Analyze:
  • Facial recognition fails on darker skin (racial bias)
  • Translation AI loses cultural context for te reo
  • Health AI trained mostly on Pākehā data
  • Predictive policing targets Māori communities

Māori Data Sovereignty (25 min)

Key Principle: Māori should control Māori data. This includes genetic data, cultural knowledge, language data used to train AI, health records, etc.
