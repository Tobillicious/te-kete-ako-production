📚 Walker Unit

The Challenge to the Narrative

Dr. Ranginui Walker - Historian, Activist, Intellectual

About This Unit

This unit explores the life and work of Dr. Ranginui Walker, a key figure in the Māori renaissance of the late 20th century. Through five interconnected lessons, students will understand how Walker challenged the dominant Pākehā narrative of New Zealand history and fought for Māori self-determination.

Unit Themes:

  • Biography: From rural teacher to leading academic and activist
  • Migration: The Great Migration and urban Māori identity
  • Activism: Ngā Tamatoa and the protest movements of the 1970s
  • Justice: The Waitangi Tribunal and Treaty settlements
  • Narrative: "Ka Whawhai Tonu Mātou" - Reclaiming Māori history
