Walker: The Challenge to the Narrative

Dr. Ranginui Walker - Historian, Activist, Intellectual

Unit Overview

This 5-lesson unit explores how Dr. Ranginui Walker challenged the dominant Pākehā narrative of New Zealand history, centering Māori perspectives and experiences. Students examine migration, activism, institutional change, and the power of storytelling.

🌿 Cultural Considerations

This unit requires cultural sensitivity and respect. Students will engage with topics including colonization, protest, and historical grievances.

Important: Before teaching, review cultural protocols with your school's Māori education liaison. Consider inviting kaumātua or community members to share perspectives.
