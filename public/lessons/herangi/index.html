About This Unit

This unit explores the extraordinary life and work of Te Puea Hērangi, a leader who embodied the spirit of the Kīngitanga movement and dedicated her life to rebuilding Māori communities.

Unit Themes:

  • Leadership: Te Puea's journey from princess to community builder
  • History: The legacy of raupatu (land confiscation) and the Waikato War
  • Resistance: Standing against WWI conscription
  • Community: Building Tūrangawaewae Marae
  • Legacy: The politics of mana and cultural revival
