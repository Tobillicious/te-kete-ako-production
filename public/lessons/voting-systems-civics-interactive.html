What is MMP?

Mixed Member Proportional System

NZ uses MMP (since 1996). Each voter gets two votes:

  • Electorate vote: Vote for local MP (72 electorates + 7 MƒÅori electorates)
  • Party vote: Vote for political party (determines overall seats)

Total: 120 seats in Parliament

The 5% Threshold

To enter Parliament, a party must either:

  • Get 5% or more of the party vote, OR
  • Win at least one electorate seat

Why? Prevents too many small parties fragmenting Parliament
