Measurement: Designing a Marae

⚡ Quick Start: Teach This Tomorrow

🕐 Prep Time: 15-20 minutes | 📄 Materials: Print handout + whiteboard

3-Step Implementation:

  1. Tonight: Read lesson plan (10 min), print handout (5 min)
  2. Tomorrow Morning: Review whakataukī and cultural context (5 min)
  3. In Class: Follow lesson sequence, adapt as needed
📏 Mathematics (Measurement)•⏱️ 60 min•👥 Year 7•🌿 Marae architecture

🎯 WALT

  • Measure length accurately (m, cm)
  • Calculate area & perimeter
  • Create scale drawings
  • Apply measurement to real design

✅ Success Criteria

  • I can measure to nearest cm
  • I can calculate area (length × width)
  • I can use scale (1:100)
  • I create accurate marae floor plan

Marae Components

  • Wharenui: Meeting house (20m × 10m)
  • Wharekai: Dining hall (15m × 8m)
  • Marae ātea: Open courtyard (30m × 25m)
  • Wharepaku: Bathrooms (6m × 4m)

Design Task (30 min)

Create scale drawing (1:100) of marae complex. Calculate total area needed. Label all buildings with dimensions.
