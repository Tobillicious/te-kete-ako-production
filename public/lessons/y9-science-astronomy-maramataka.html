Moon Phases & Science (25 min)

8 Moon Phases: New Moon → Waxing Crescent → First Quarter → Waxing Gibbous → Full Moon → Waning Gibbous → Last Quarter → Waning Crescent

Science: Moon doesn't make light - reflects Sun's light. We see different amounts depending on Moon's position relative to Earth & Sun.

Maramataka System (30 min)

Maramataka: Māori lunar calendar with 30 moon phases (days), each with a name and meaning. Used to guide:

  • Planting crops (best days for planting)
  • Fishing (when fish abundant)
  • Harvesting (optimal times)
  • Important decisions (good/bad days)
  • Health & wellbeing (energy levels)
Example Phases:
  • Whiro: New moon - poor day, rest/reflect
  • Rākaunui: Full moon - excellent day, lots of energy
  • Tangaroa: Good for fishing

Both Systems Valid! (10 min)

Western astronomy explains HOW (physics, light, orbits). Maramataka explains WHEN & WHY (timing, connection to environment). Both are science - observation + pattern recognition over centuries!
