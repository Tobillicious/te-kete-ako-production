    • Approach Māori content with respect and openness
    • Recognize students' diverse cultural backgrounds and experiences
    • Create space for Māori students to share their perspectives
    • Consult with whānau and local iwi for culturally sensitive topics

    🌟 Our Mission

    Te Kete Ako exists to support kaiako (teachers) in Aotearoa New Zealand with culturally responsive, high-quality educational resources that honor Te Ao Māori while meeting modern pedagogical standards. We believe every student deserves access to engaging, culturally authentic learning experiences that connect them to their identity and prepare them for the future.

    🏛️ Our Vision

    To create an educational ecosystem where Te Ao Māori is naturally integrated into all learning, where technology enhances rather than replaces human connection, and where every kaiako has access to resources that inspire, engage, and empower their students to achieve their potential.

    🌿 Our Values

    🌺 Manaakitanga (Care)

    ⚖️ Tika (Integrity)

    🚀 Auaha (Innovation)
