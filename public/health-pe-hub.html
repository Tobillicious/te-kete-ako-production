🏠 Te Whare Tapa Whā - The Four-Sided House

Our hauora (wellbeing) curriculum is built on Te Whare Tapa Whā, developed by Sir Mason Durie. Like a wharenui (meeting house), our wellbeing needs four strong walls to stand.

💪

Taha Tinana

Physical Wellbeing

  • Physical fitness & activity
  • Nutrition & healthy eating
  • Sleep & rest
  • Body awareness
🧠

Taha Hinengaro

Mental & Emotional Wellbeing

  • Emotional regulation
  • Resilience & coping
  • Self-awareness
  • Mental health literacy
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Taha Whānau

Social Wellbeing

  • Relationships & connections
  • Belonging & identity
  • Communication skills
  • Community participation
✨

Taha Wairua

Spiritual Wellbeing

  • Purpose & meaning
  • Values & beliefs
  • Connection to nature
  • Cultural identity
