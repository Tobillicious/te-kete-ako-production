❓ Common Questions

🤔 Do I need to create an account?

No! All resources are freely accessible without signup. Create an account later if you want to save favorites or track your classes.

📱 Can I use this on my classroom tablets?

Absolutely! Te Kete Ako is optimized for iPads and Chromebooks. Students can access resources directly on their devices.

🌿 How is cultural content integrated?

Every resource includes whakataukī, tikanga protocols, and Te Ao Māori perspectives naturally woven into the content - not as add-ons, but as fundamental to the learning.

💰 Is this really free?

Yes! Te Kete Ako is completely free for all NZ teachers and students. Our mission is to "Fill the basket of knowledge" for everyone.

🧠 What is GraphRAG?

GraphRAG is our AI knowledge graph that maps connections between resources. It suggests related lessons, shows cross-curricular links, and helps you discover the perfect resource for your students.

📞 How do I get help?

Every page has a help button (bottom right). You can also email us or join our teacher community for support and collaboration.
