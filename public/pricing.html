Frequently Asked Questions

What is GraphRAG?

GraphRAG (Graph Retrieval-Augmented Generation) is our intelligent knowledge system that maps 19,696 resources and 231,257 relationships. It learns from usage patterns and improves recommendations automatically.

How does the AI work?

We integrate GLM (DeepSeek) AI with our GraphRAG system to provide intelligent lesson planning, content generation, and cultural context. The AI reasons over all 19,696 resources to give you the best recommendations.

What are teaching variants?

Every lesson offers 2-3 pedagogical approaches (Inquiry-Based, Collaborative, Guided, Project-Based, Experiential), each aligned with Te Ao Māori principles. Choose the approach that fits your class.

Is this aligned with NZ Curriculum?

Yes! All content is designed for the New Zealand Curriculum with Te Ao Māori cultural integration. We honor the principles of Whanaungatanga, Manaakitanga, Kaitiakitanga, Rangatiratanga, Kotahitanga, and Ako.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Individual plans can be cancelled anytime. School plans have a 30-day money-back guarantee. No lock-in contracts.

What support do you provide?

Individual: Email support. School: Priority email + video tutorials. Enterprise: 24/7 support + dedicated account manager.
