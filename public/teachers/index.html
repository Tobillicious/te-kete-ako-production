    🏛️ Connection to Mangakōtukutuku College Values

    Whaiara: Rising Up - Taking action to create positive change

    whaiara by encouraging students to engage with content in ways that embody this value.

    • Approach Māori content with respect and openness
    • Recognize students' diverse cultural backgrounds and experiences
    • Create space for Māori students to share their perspectives
    • Consult with whānau and local iwi for culturally sensitive topics

    🌿 Cultural Integration

    All resources integrate mātauranga Māori authentically, not as an add-on. Cultural protocols, te reo Māori, and Māori perspectives are woven throughout the content.

    • Te reo Māori vocabulary and concepts
    • Whakataukī (Māori proverbs) in context
    • Cultural protocols and tikanga
    • NZ Curriculum Achievement Objectives
    • Differentiation for all learners
