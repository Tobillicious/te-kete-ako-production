üìñ How to Use These Templates

Method 1: Copy & Fill (Recommended)

  1. Click "Download" on the template you need
  2. Save to appropriate directory (/handouts/, /lessons/, or /units/)
  3. Replace all [PLACEHOLDERS] with your content
  4. Update CSS paths if needed (add ../ for subdirectories)
  5. Fill in cultural elements (whakataukƒ´, Te Reo)
  6. Test locally at http://localhost:8001

Method 2: View & Reference

  1. Click "View" to see the template in action
  2. Use browser's "View Source" to see HTML structure
  3. Copy the sections you need into your existing file
  4. Adapt to your specific needs
