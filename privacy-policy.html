🛡️ Privacy Policy

How we protect, use, and respect your information at Te Kete Ako

📅 Last Updated

July 2025 | Effective immediately

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Te Kete Ako is an educational platform designed to serve ākonga (students) and kaiako (teachers) of Mangakōtukutuku College and the wider educational community. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing culturally-grounded, high-quality educational resources.

Our promise: Your personal information will only be used to enhance your learning experience and will never be sold or shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

2. Information We Collect

📧 Account Information

  • Email address: For login and account recovery
  • Name: To personalize your experience
  • Role: Student, Teacher, or Caregiver (to provide appropriate content)
  • School affiliation: If provided, to connect you with your learning community

📚 Learning Data

  • Progress tracking: Which resources you've accessed
  • Project submissions: Work you submit through the platform
  • Preferences: Settings and customizations you make
  • Usage patterns: How you navigate and use the platform (to improve our service)

💻 Technical Information

  • Device information: Browser type, device type (to ensure compatibility)
  • Log data: Access times, pages visited (for security and performance)
  • Cookies: To remember your preferences and keep you logged in

3. How We Use Your Information

🎓 Educational Purposes

  • Provide personalized learning recommendations
  • Track progress and achievement
  • Enable collaboration between students and teachers
  • Facilitate project submissions and feedback

🔧 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Ensure platform security and performance

📬 Communication

  • Send important updates about the platform
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Notify you of new resources relevant to your interests
  • Account security notifications

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access controls: Only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular audits: We regularly review our security practices
  • Secure hosting: Data is stored with reputable, security-focused providers
  • Privacy by design: We collect only what's necessary for our service

5. Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your personal information:

👁️ Access

View all personal data we have about you

✏️ Correct

Update or correct your personal information

📱 Export

Download your data in a portable format

🗑️ Delete

Permanently remove your account and data

To exercise these rights: Contact us at privacy@tekete.education or use the data management tools in your account settings.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With teachers and schools: If you're a student, your teachers may see your progress and submissions
  • Service providers: Trusted partners who help us operate the platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety of our users
  • Anonymous data: We may share aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics for research

7. Special Protections for Students

🧒 Under 13 Years Old

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

  • Parental rights: Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information
  • Educational use only: Student data is used solely for educational purposes
  • No advertising: We don't use student data for advertising or marketing
  • Data minimization: We collect only what's necessary for the educational service

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential cookies: Keep you logged in and remember your preferences
  • Analytics cookies: Understand how you use the platform to make improvements
  • Performance cookies: Help us optimize loading times and functionality

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect platform functionality.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll notify you by email and/or prominently on the platform
  • We'll give you at least 30 days notice before significant changes take effect
  • You can review the updated policy before it becomes effective
  • Continued use of the platform means you accept the new policy

Questions about privacy, data protection, or this policy? We're here to help:
