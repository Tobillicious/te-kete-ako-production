🤖 AI-Powered Insights

Learning Style Analysis

The system automatically analyzes student behavior patterns to identify learning preferences:

  • Visual/Reading Learners: Prefer handouts and written materials
  • Kinesthetic/Interactive Learners: Thrive with games and hands-on activities
  • Auditory/Visual Learners: Benefit from video content and multimedia
  • Multimodal Learners: Engage well with diverse approaches

Predictive Recommendations

Get personalized suggestions for:

  • Next learning activities based on current progress
  • Curriculum gaps that need attention
  • Cultural integration opportunities
  • Teaching strategy adaptations
🌟 Cultural Intelligence: The system includes specific recommendations for integrating Te Ao Māori perspectives and mātauranga Māori approaches.

🔧 Technical Integration

Seamless Operation

The analytics system works automatically across all Te Kete Ako resources:

  • Handouts: Track reading time, scroll depth, and engagement
  • Games: Monitor gameplay, score progression, and cultural bonus points
  • Lessons: Analyze activity completion and assessment interactions
  • Videos: Track YouTube resource access and educational content viewing

Privacy & Data Security

All analytics data is stored locally in the browser. No personal information is transmitted or stored externally. Teachers maintain complete control over their classroom data.

🛡️ Privacy First: This system respects student privacy while providing valuable educational insights. Perfect for schools with strict data protection requirements.
