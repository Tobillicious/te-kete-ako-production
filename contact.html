Contribute Resources | Tuku Rauemi

Are you a teacher with great resources to share? Te Kete Ako is built by the teaching community, for the teaching community. We welcome contributions that align with our values of cultural authenticity, curriculum alignment, and research-based pedagogy.

If you have unit plans, lesson sequences, or teaching materials you'd like to share, please contact us at:

✨ contribute@teketeako.nz

All contributed resources remain your intellectual property. Contributors are acknowledged and can request removal at any time.

Response Time | Wā Whakautu

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2-3 business days. During school holidays or peak times, responses may take slightly longer. We appreciate your patience and understanding!
