📖 Key Statistical Terms

Mode: Most common value

Range: Difference between highest and lowest

Total: Sum of all values

Category: Group of similar items

Rerekētanga (Differentiation)

For Support: In a group, create a poster for one of the indicators, showing the connection between the sign and the environmental condition.

For Extension: Research a local environmental project that is using both mātauranga Māori and modern science. Summarise its goals and methods.
