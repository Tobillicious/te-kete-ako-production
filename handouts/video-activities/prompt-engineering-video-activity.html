Video Activity: Advanced Prompt Engineering

An Expert Deep Dive with Sander Schulhoff

Watch & Reflect

Watch the video "Prompt Engineering and AI Red Teaming" by Sander Schulhoff (link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_BRhRh7mOX0). This is an advanced talk for those with a strong interest. As you watch, consider the questions below.

Critical Thinking Questions

1. Schulhoff talks about "prompt injection". In your own words, what is this? Why is it a security risk for AI applications?

2. What is "AI Red Teaming"? Why is it important for companies like Google and OpenAI to do this?

3. (Extension) This talk is from 2023. The field of AI moves incredibly fast. Research one new development in prompt engineering or AI safety that has occurred since this video was made.
